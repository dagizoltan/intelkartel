<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Responsible Tiny Government Solution:</h3>
<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">A responsible, tiny government is one that operates under strict adherence to the rule of law, ensures equal protection for all citizens, and fosters an environment where personal freedoms and civil liberties are respected. The role of government should be to maintain justice, provide necessary public services (like defense, healthcare, and education), and regulate to protect human rights without overreach. It should ensure transparency, accountability, and participation, creating checks on executive power to prevent abuses. A smaller, more efficient government—one focused on protecting individuals rather than controlling them—would reduce the frequency of both horrid and everyday crimes while empowering communities to thrive and self-organize responsibly.</p>
