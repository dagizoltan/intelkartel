<h1 class="wp-block-heading">UNIT COMMANDER — ONE-PAGE ROE CARD (WARTIME CIVIL SUPPORT)</h1>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Purpose:</strong> Rapid, legally-compliant guide for military units temporarily supporting civilian law enforcement. Use this card during planning, <strong>execution</strong> and briefbacks. Follow written activation order and legal brief — this card summarizes core limits and immediate actions.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">AUTHORIZATION (MANDATORY)</h2>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>No action</strong> without: written civil authority request + signed military activation order.</li>
<li><strong>Order must include:</strong> mission area, objectives, start/end time (sunset), legal basis, assigned liaison.</li>
<li><strong>Legal officer on-call</strong> for questions; copy ROE to all patrol/leads before movement.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">PRINCIPLES (ABSOLUTE)</h2>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Civilian police retain primary authority. Military supports only.</li>
<li>Use of force: <strong>Necessary — Proportionate — Last resort.</strong></li>
<li>All uses of force causing serious injury/death → immediate independent investigation.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">ESCALATION OF FORCE (IN ORDER)</h2>
<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Presence &amp; verbal commands</strong> (local language) — warn &amp; order compliance.</li>
<li><strong>Hands-on / restraint / containment</strong> — effect arrest where feasible.</li>
<li><strong>Less-lethal</strong> (batons, shields, stun devices, area denial, dispersal).</li>
<li><strong>Intermediate munitions</strong> (rubber/beanbag) — planned, controlled, medics ready.</li>
<li><strong>Lethal force</strong> — <strong>ONLY</strong> to stop an <strong>imminent</strong> threat to life/serious bodily harm and when no other option exists.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>NEVER</strong> issue or follow blanket “shoot-to-kill” orders.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">LETHAL FORCE — RED LINE CHECKLIST (ALL 5 REQUIRED)</h2>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>☑ Threat is imminent (active use of lethal weapon / explosive).</li>
<li>☑ No reasonable, less-harmful alternative exists.</li>
<li>☑ Action is proportionate to the threat.</li>
<li>☑ Target is positively identified as direct threat (not bystander).</li>
<li>☑ Incident will be reported immediately (see Reporting).</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">If any box is unchecked — do <strong>not</strong> use lethal force.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">ARRESTS, DETENTION &amp; HANDOVER</h2>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Military detentions must be <strong>transferred to police ASAP</strong> (within legal max timeframe).</li>
<li>Provide detainee access to medical care and legal counsel.</li>
<li>Maintain chain-of-custody for evidence; use police evidence forms; hand over to prosecutors.</li>
<li>No unilateral property/asset confiscation without a court order.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">PLANNED RAIDS / HIGH-RISK ACTIONS</h2>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Require: civilian liaison + prosecutor sign-off + warrants where practicable.</li>
<li>Pre-brief: objectives, use-of-force thresholds, entry team roles, medic evac plan, evidence preservation.</li>
<li>Sniper marksmen only for hostage rescue / imminent-lethal-threat — under strict authorization.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">MEDICAL &amp; HUMANITY</h2>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Immediate first aid to wounded (friend/foe/civilian).</li>
<li>MedEvac priority = save life. Document treatment and chain of custody.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">RECORDING &amp; EVIDENCE</h2>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Record all operations (body-cam / vehicle / field log). Preserve footage for investigations.</li>
<li>Tag &amp; log all collected items; maintain detailed chain-of-custody forms.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">REPORTING (IMMEDIATE)</h2>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Any serious injury/death or property damage → <strong>stop</strong> operation if safe; notify Joint Civil-Military Ops Center within <strong>30 minutes</strong>.</li>
<li>Submit initial incident report within <strong>4 hours</strong>, full report within <strong>72 hours</strong>. Include: who, what, when, where, how, witnesses, evidence, and legal basis.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">OVERSIGHT &amp; ACCOUNTABILITY</h2>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Expect independent oversight, parliamentary review, and judicial inquiry.</li>
<li>Violations of ROE = criminal and disciplinary consequences.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">COMMAND CONTACTS (fill before sortie)</h2>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Civil Liaison (name / tel): __________________</li>
<li>Legal Officer (name / tel): __________________</li>
<li>Joint Ops Center (tel / call sign): __________________</li>
<li>Medical Evac (call sign / tel): __________________</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">QUICK REMINDERS (KEEP VISIBLE)</h2>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>No punishment, property seizure, or extra-judicial action without law.</li>
<li>Protect civilians, journalists, medical staff, and judges.</li>
<li>Preserve evidence to ensure prosecutions — decisive force without prosecution benefits no one.</li>
</ul>
