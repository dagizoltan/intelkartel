<h2 class="wp-block-heading">🎲 <strong>DICE-ONLY GAMES (Using Standard 6-Sided Dice)</strong></h2>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">1. <strong>Pig</strong> (1 die)</h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 2+<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Reach 100 points.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>On your turn, roll the die.</li>
<li>If it’s <strong>2–6</strong>, add it to your turn total and choose to roll again or “hold” (keep points).</li>
<li>If you roll a <strong>1</strong>, you lose all points from this turn and end your turn.</li>
<li>First to 100 wins.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">2. <strong>Beat That!</strong> (2 dice)</h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 2+<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Make the highest number.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Roll 2 dice. Form the biggest 2-digit number (e.g., roll 3 and 6 → 63).</li>
<li>Highest number wins the round.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">3. <strong>Cee-Lo</strong> (3 dice)</h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 2+<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Roll better than your opponents.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Aim for:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>4-5-6 = automatic win</li>
<li>1-2-3 = automatic loss</li>
<li>Triples = higher triple wins (e.g., 3-3-3 beats 2-2-2)</li>
<li>Pair + another = single counts (5-5-2 → 2)</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Highest single wins if no one gets better.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">4. <strong>Ship, Captain, and Crew</strong> (5 dice)</h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 2+<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Roll a 6 (ship), 5 (captain), 4 (crew) in order, then highest cargo (remaining 2 dice).<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Roll up to 3 times.</li>
<li>You must roll and “keep” 6 first, then 5, then 4.</li>
<li>Remaining 2 dice = cargo.</li>
<li>Highest cargo wins.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">5. <strong>Liar’s Dice</strong> (1 die + cup per player)</h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 2+<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Bluff or catch a bluff.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Everyone rolls and hides their die.</li>
<li>Take turns guessing how many total of a face (e.g., “three 4s”).</li>
<li>Next player must raise or call “liar.”</li>
<li>Reveal and check. Loser loses a die (if playing with more dice).</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">6. <strong>Farkle</strong> (6 dice)</h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 2+<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Reach 10,000 points.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>On your turn, roll 6 dice.</li>
<li>Score combos: 1 = 100 pts, 5 = 50 pts, 3 of a kind = 100×face value (except 1s = 1000), etc.</li>
<li>Keep scoring dice and reroll rest.</li>
<li>If you score nothing, it’s a <strong>Farkle</strong> — lose turn.</li>
<li>Hold or risk rerolling.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">7. <strong>Yahtzee</strong> (5 dice)</h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 1+<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Score best combinations.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Roll 3 times per turn, choosing which dice to keep.</li>
<li>Score in categories: three-of-a-kind, full house, small straight, Yahtzee (5 of a kind), etc.</li>
<li>One entry per category.</li>
<li>Highest total score wins.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">🃏 <strong>CARD-ONLY GAMES (Standard 52-card Deck)</strong></h2>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">1. <strong>War</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 2<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Win all the cards.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Split deck evenly.</li>
<li>Each player flips top card. Higher card wins both.</li>
<li>Tie = “war”: place 3 down, 1 up. Winner takes all.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">2. <strong>Snap</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 2+<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Win all the cards.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Take turns flipping top card into a pile.</li>
<li>If two consecutive cards match, yell “Snap!” first to win pile.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">3. <strong>Go Fish</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 2–5<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Collect sets of 4.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Ask others for cards (“Do you have any 7s?”)</li>
<li>If yes, they give them to you; if no, you “go fish” (draw).</li>
<li>Form sets of 4 cards.</li>
<li>Most sets wins.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">4. <strong>Old Maid</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 2–6<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Avoid the unmatched card.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Remove one queen.</li>
<li>Deal all cards.</li>
<li>Discard pairs.</li>
<li>Take turns drawing from the next player’s hand.</li>
<li>Person left with odd card (Old Maid) loses.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">5. <strong>Cheat / I Doubt It</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 3+<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Get rid of all cards.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Play face-down cards and say the rank (e.g., “two 7s”).</li>
<li>Others can challenge by saying “cheat!”</li>
<li>If you lied, pick up pile; if not, challenger does.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">6. <strong>Crazy Eights</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 2–4<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Get rid of all cards.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Match top card of discard pile by number or suit.</li>
<li>Play an 8 to change suit.</li>
<li>Draw if you can’t play.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">7. <strong>Blackjack</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 2+<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Get as close to 21 without going over.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Face cards = 10, Ace = 1 or 11.</li>
<li>Each player vs dealer.</li>
<li>“Hit” (draw) or “stand” (stay).</li>
<li>Over 21 = bust. Closest to 21 wins.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">8. <strong>Memory / Concentration</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 1+<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Match pairs.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Lay cards face down.</li>
<li>Flip two per turn. Match = keep them.</li>
<li>Most matches wins.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">9. <strong>Speed / Spit</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 2<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Get rid of cards fast.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Both play simultaneously onto center piles.</li>
<li>Play cards that are 1 higher or lower.</li>
<li>Draw from personal pile when needed.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">10. <strong>President / Asshole</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 4–7<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Get rid of all cards.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Play cards equal or higher than last played.</li>
<li>Clear pile if no one can play.</li>
<li>Next round: ranks determine seating and card passing.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">11. <strong>Rummy</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 2–4<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Form sets and runs.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Draw, discard each turn.</li>
<li>Meld 3+ cards of a kind or in sequence.</li>
<li>First to discard all wins.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">12. <strong>Gin Rummy</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 2<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Form melds and go “gin.”<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Same as Rummy but only 2 players.</li>
<li>Knock (end round) if deadwood (non-meld cards) ≤ 10.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">13. <strong>Hearts</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 4<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Avoid hearts and Queen of Spades.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Trick-taking game.</li>
<li>Most hearts/Queen = penalty points.</li>
<li>First to 100 loses; lowest wins.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">14. <strong>Spades</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 4 (teams)<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Win tricks based on bids.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Bid how many tricks you’ll win.</li>
<li>Spades trump all suits.</li>
<li>Score based on success/failure.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">15. <strong>Bridge</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Players:</strong> 4 (teams)<br/><strong>Goal:</strong> Win tricks based on contract.<br/><strong>How to Play:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Complex bidding and play.</li>
<li>Trick-taking with partnership and deep strategy.</li>
</ul>
