<li><strong>Hebrew</strong>: תודה! (Toda!)</li>

<li><strong>Russian</strong>: Большое спасибо! (Bol’shoye spasibo!)</li>
<li><strong>Hebrew</strong>: תודה רבה! (Toda raba!)</li>
