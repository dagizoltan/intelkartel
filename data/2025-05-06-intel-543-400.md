<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>10 Steps to Become a Siloviki Father of Tomorrow, Today</strong></h2>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>1. Master the Strategic Map</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Learn geography</strong> like your life depends on it. Understand every key location: from the <strong>Caucasus to Kaliningrad</strong>, from <strong>Belarus to the Bering Strait</strong>, and even the <strong>North Pole and American theater</strong>.</li>
<li>Trace resources, choke points, and supply lines.</li>
<li>Redraw the world in your mind with Moscow as the strategic heart.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>2. Train the Body Like a Warrior</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Run. Crawl. Climb. Fall and rise again.</li>
<li>Practice <strong>combat sports</strong>: wrestling, boxing, sambo.</li>
<li>Learn to evade and strike fast like a <strong>Spetsnaz ghost</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>3. Draw, Analyze, Repeat</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Draw the map</strong> from memory daily.</li>
<li>Mark natural resources, troop corridors, and ideological fault lines.</li>
<li>Build your own <strong>military atlas</strong>—a holy book of geo-strategy.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>4. Be Resourceful—and Fearlessly Clever</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Use <strong>everything at hand</strong>. Turn a spoon into a compass. Turn a joke into morale.</li>
<li>Combine <strong>ingenuity</strong> with humor. A silovik must survive in chaos and thrive in boredom.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>5. Make Strategic Jokes</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Every joke must hide a lesson.</li>
<li>Example: <em>“Why did the tank cross the border? Because the map said it could.”</em></li>
<li>Humor is not weakness—it is <strong>weaponized morale</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>6. Think Like a General</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Always ask: “Where’s the opportunity?” and “Where’s the trap?”</li>
<li>Learn from <strong>Sun Tzu, Suvorov, Dugin, and Machiavelli</strong>.</li>
<li>Run <strong>mental war games</strong> before bed and after waking up.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>7. Read the Big Book &amp; the Mashkirova Manual Twice Daily</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Read once in the morning for direction, again at night for reflection.</li>
<li>Know every <strong>paragraph, tactic, and moral code</strong>.</li>
<li>Let these manuals be your <strong>mental armor</strong> and <strong>ideological compass</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>8. Develop a Meticulous Eye</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Observe everything: uniforms, maps, faces, patrol routes, mistakes.</li>
<li>Train with <strong>spot-the-difference games</strong> and reconnaissance drills.</li>
<li>A future leader must <strong>notice the invisible</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>9. Look Sharp as the Devil</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Appearance is discipline.</li>
<li>Boots shined, uniform crisp, eyes focused.</li>
<li>Your walk should tell others: <em>“I command fate.”</em></li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>10. Believe and Achieve Through Relentless Activity</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>No idle hands. No wasted minutes.</li>
<li>Train, study, laugh, write, draw, read, strategize.</li>
<li><strong>Victory belongs to the active</strong>—those who burn with purpose <strong>day and night</strong>.</li>
</ul>
