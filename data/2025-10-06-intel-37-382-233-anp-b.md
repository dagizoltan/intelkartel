<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Operational Allegations</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">

<li><strong>Government Collusion:</strong><br/>Certain officials allegedly provide protection, access, and funding channels for covert operations.</li>
<li><strong>Hidden Dynastic Influence:</strong><br/>Transnational elite elements purportedly oversee logistics, command, and long-term objectives beyond state oversight.</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Operation Jupiter / Uranus Objectives (Alleged)</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Manage covert factions and distribute roles during global elections and economic cycles.</li>
<li>Funnel and conceal resources for continuity of influence.</li>
<li>Coordinate post-event redistribution of “treasure” and assets across aligned cells.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>The “American North” Framework</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Alleged covert infrastructure described as the <strong>“American North”</strong> or <strong>“Inside Pyramid”</strong>, acting as a fallback command for U.S. and allied systems.</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Reports suggest systemic disappearance of institutionalized children (speculatively 400,000+).</li>
<li>Said to be part of a “forever resource” mechanism sustaining long-term covert capabilities.</li>
</ul>

<li>Government and private partnerships allegedly underpin deep-state contingencies.</li>
