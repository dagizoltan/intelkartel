<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">Estimating the total number of individuals legally carrying firearms worldwide as part of military, police, or intelligence agencies is challenging due to variations in the size, scope, and classification of such personnel across different countries. However, here’s an approximation based on available data:</p>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">1. <strong>Military Personnel</strong>:</h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>The <strong>global military personnel</strong> (active duty) is estimated at around <strong>20 million</strong> people. This includes all branches of the military, from army to air force and navy. Each of these personnel is typically armed as part of their service.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">2. <strong>Police Forces</strong>:</h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>The <strong>global police force</strong> is estimated to have around <strong>10 million</strong> officers. The exact number varies greatly depending on the country. Some countries like China have huge police forces, while others, such as smaller nations, have smaller forces. These officers are legally armed as part of their law enforcement duties.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">3. <strong>Intelligence Agencies</strong>:</h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Intelligence agencies such as the CIA, MI6, FSB, and others employ personnel that may legally carry firearms for operational and protective purposes. The number of intelligence officers is less transparent, but estimates suggest it may range between <strong>500,000 to 1 million</strong> globally, considering all countries.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">4. <strong>Other Armed Security Personnel</strong>:</h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Additionally, there are other categories of armed personnel, such as private security firms hired to protect critical infrastructure, government buildings, and diplomatic missions. This could add <strong>several million</strong> individuals globally.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Total Estimate:</h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Combining these categories:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Military</strong>: ~20 million</li>
<li><strong>Police</strong>: ~10 million</li>
<li><strong>Intelligence</strong>: ~500,000 – 1 million</li>
<li><strong>Private Security/Other Armed Personnel</strong>: ~2-5 million</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">A rough estimate for the total number of individuals legally carrying firearms as part of military, police, and intelligence services worldwide could be in the ballpark of <strong>30 to 40 million</strong> individuals.</p>
<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">This figure can vary based on the size of the forces in individual countries and their classification of personnel (e.g., paramilitary, reserves). Keep in mind that these numbers are approximations, as not all countries publish detailed or accurate data on their armed services and police forces.</p>
