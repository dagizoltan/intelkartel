<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This memorandum outlines a bleak but increasingly substantiated scenario: the internal decay of democratic institutions caused by the intentional and systemic rotation of ideologically loyal yet professionally unfit personnel into government roles. It has become weaponized, producing generations of compromised operatives, institutional saboteurs, and ideological loyalists who erode national cohesion from within.</p>

The majority of these roles are filled not by competence, but by loyalty to ideology, donors, or identity-based factions.</p>

</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Maintaining Perpetual Crisis for Power Expansion:</strong>
<ul class="wp-block-list">

</ul>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Unofficial coalitions of former cabinet members, agency heads, and advisors maintain influence through soft coercion, NGO pipelines, and donor leverage. They act as a “sleeper” command structure during changes in administration.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
