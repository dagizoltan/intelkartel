<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

Intelligence indicates that survival is plausible for those strategically positioned inland and at elevated altitudes. This follow-up report serves as a relocation directive to maximize survival and ensure the continuity of witty commentary.</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">

<li>Timing: Wave ETA is classified but “soonish.” If you can read this, you still have time.</li>
</ol>
