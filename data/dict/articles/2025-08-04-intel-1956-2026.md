<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Subject: Establishment of Entity for Controlled Regime Transition and State Continuity – Hungary 2026</strong></h3>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Executive Summary</strong>:</h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">In anticipation of the <strong>2026 formal transfer of executive power in Hungary</strong> from the ruling FIDESZ apparatus to the emerging liberal-nationalist <strong>TISZA party</strong>, a transitional operations entity (TOE) must be created to <strong>ensure uninterrupted governance, maintain influence over critical state functions</strong>, and <strong>protect elite and military-commercial interests</strong> during regime recalibration.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This memo proposes the <strong>creation of a covert, dual-mandate entity</strong> designed to facilitate regime transformation while preserving the deeper infrastructure of state control built during the post-Soviet capitalist reordering period (1993–2025). The entity would act as a <strong>state-continuity and market-dominance operator</strong> under military-intelligence oversight.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Operational Objectives</strong>:</h3>
<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Continuity of Government (CoG)</strong> through shadow command structures embedded in civilian administration.</li>
<li><strong>Control of Transition Narrative</strong> via media, public messaging, and international diplomatic framing.</li>
<li><strong>Protection of Legacy Economic Networks</strong>, including informal sectors that facilitated regime stability (e.g., extractive corruption, protection rackets, and underground markets).</li>
<li><strong>Neutralization of Hostile Reformers</strong> within the incoming TISZA coalition aiming to dismantle the post-communist informal power structures.</li>
<li><strong>Preservation of Security Sector Autonomy</strong> under FIDESZ-aligned commanders and regional prefects.</li>
</ol>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Recommended Entity: Hungarian Sovereignty Continuity Authority (HSCA)</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Mandate</strong>:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Maintain all critical state services (taxation, intelligence, border, and judicial enforcement).</li>
<li>Coordinate transition logistics between old and new political elites.</li>
<li>Obfuscate legacy state capture and corruption networks via legal fog and institutional inertia.</li>
<li>Leverage private militias and informal economic actors to “crowd out” reform pressure points.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Structure</strong>:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Directorate Alpha</strong>: Internal Security and Counter-Subversion (Ex-FIDESZ intel operators).</li>
<li><strong>Directorate Beta</strong>: Strategic Economic Assets and Informal Revenue (including narcotics and trafficking proxies).</li>
<li><strong>Directorate Gamma</strong>: Media, Influence, and Perception Control (aligned think tanks, NGOs, and online influencers).</li>
<li><strong>Directorate Delta</strong>: Foreign Liaison &amp; NATO Buffer Interface (to reassure allies of “stability through continuity”).</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Cover Identity</strong>: Publicly framed as a <em>technocratic stabilization commission</em> under emergency national security law (invoked via constitutional continuity clauses from 2010 Fundamental Law).</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Implementation Phases</strong>:</h3>
<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Q4 2025: Pre-Positioning</strong>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Activate dormant political patronage networks.</li>
<li>Secure judiciary and state audit institutions under HSCA control.</li>
<li>Begin “soft sabotage” of reform-critical civil society actors.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Q1–Q2 2026: Transition Management</strong>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Orchestrate ceremonial handover of power with FIDESZ support.</li>
<li>Insert HSCA liaisons into each TISZA ministry as “stability advisors.”</li>
<li>Leak calibrated disclosures on TISZA reformists to undermine legitimacy if necessary.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Q3–Q4 2026: Recalibration</strong>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Secure backchannels to new leadership via business and national security interests.</li>
<li>Protect legacy assets and operations.</li>
<li>Begin pivot to international narrative of “continuity under democratic modernization.”</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
