<h1 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Call to Establish the Hungarian People’s Senate (HPS)</strong></h1>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><em>A New Tier of Democratic Governance for a Sovereign, Strategic, and Science-Led Hungary</em></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Issued by:</strong> Strategic Futures Council<br/><strong>Drafted for Public Review</strong><br/><strong>Date:</strong> June 2025</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>I. Introduction</strong></h2>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">In an age of unprecedented technological acceleration, regional volatility, and global realignment, Hungary stands at a crossroads. To realize its full potential as a sovereign, secure, and strategically-minded nation, we must evolve our democratic architecture. It is time to <strong>establish a new legislative organ</strong>: the <strong>Hungarian People’s Senate (HPS)</strong>.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This Senate will serve as a <strong>permanent civic chamber</strong>, integrating <strong>all Hungarian citizens aged 30 and above</strong> into national deliberation, decision-making, and long-term policy shaping. It is a house of wisdom, service, and ambition — not of elites, but of engaged citizens.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>II. Structure of the Senate</strong></h2>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Membership:</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Every <strong>Hungarian citizen aged 30+</strong> becomes a <strong>voting member</strong> of the Senate.</li>
<li>Citizens abroad and digital citizens are included via verified identity protocols.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Participation Tools:</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Every member receives:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>A <strong>Democracy Television Set</strong> (DTV)</li>
<li>An <strong>Intelligent Handheld Remote</strong> (IHR), voice-activated, AI-assisted</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>These tools enable:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Proposal review and voting</li>
<li>Interactive debates and policy workshops</li>
<li>Issue-based caucusing</li>
<li>Access to <strong>5,700+ democracy channels</strong> — each linked to public think tanks, factions, and forums</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Senate Sessions:</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Held <strong>daily in decentralized form</strong>, with secure voting windows</li>
<li>Monthly <strong>National Synthesis Week</strong>: key bills are finalized, reviewed, or rejected</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>III. Mission and Guiding Principles</strong></h2>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This Senate is a <strong>non-partisan, science-driven, and merit-based civic infrastructure</strong>, guided by:</p>
<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Science</strong> – Empirical policy, technocratic vision, and data-informed strategy</li>
<li><strong>Humanism</strong> – Inclusion, equity, and deep moral reasoning</li>
<li><strong>Merit</strong> – Reward and responsibility tied to service, contribution, and capability</li>
</ol>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>IV. Economic and National Objectives</strong></h2>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The Senate will directly deliberate and vote on:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Macroeconomic planning</strong>: industrial zones, tax reforms, future investments</li>
<li><strong>Population strategy</strong>: pro-natal incentives, urban housing, digital migration</li>
<li><strong>Infrastructure growth</strong>: energy, rail, tech grids, smart cities</li>
<li><strong>Military development</strong>: NATO-aligned regional force projection and deterrence</li>
<li><strong>Diplomacy &amp; alliances</strong>: reforge transatlantic ties, regional leadership, EU reform</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>V. Senate Compensation Structure</strong></h2>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">To professionalize the civic role and honor national service:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Golden Parachute Retirement Package</strong>:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Between <strong>$3 million to $7 million USD</strong> issued to long-term contributors by 2050</li>
<li>Funded by sovereign wealth revenue and the $500/month national contribution model</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>VI. Resource Pooling and National Dialogue</strong></h2>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Through the DTV/IHR system, citizens will:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Propose and vote on national projects (e.g., “Build the Eastern Rail Arc”, “Solar Village Plan”)</li>
<li>Watch and analyze policy media from <strong>multiple ideological channels</strong></li>
<li>Establish <strong>Factional News Networks</strong> — government-funded but publicly run, with:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>At least <strong>5,700 channels</strong>, each with a distinct voice and political theory</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Conduct <strong>real-time polling</strong>, audits, and project tracking for state activities</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>VII. Constitutional Legitimacy</strong></h2>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This Senate does not replace Parliament. It adds a <strong>fourth tier</strong> to the Hungarian state:</p>
<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Legislative</li>
<li>Executive</li>
<li>Judicial</li>
<li><strong>Civic-Strategic</strong> (People’s Senate)</li>
</ol>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">It is built into the long-term framework of Hungary’s post-2030 <strong>Constitutional Revision for Sovereignty, Resilience, and Regional Leadership.</strong></p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>VIII. Conclusion: Rise of the Intelligent Republic</strong></h2>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Hungary has the cultural depth, intellectual brilliance, and geopolitical will to become a <strong>leading node in the 21st-century democratic world</strong>. The Hungarian People’s Senate will be the engine of this transformation — where democracy meets technology, wisdom meets scale, and citizen meets state in a dialogue of strength.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Let the <strong>Republic rise</strong>, one vote, one channel, one citizen at a time.</p>
