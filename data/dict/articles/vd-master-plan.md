<h3 class="wp-block-heading"></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Total HUNGARIAN Budget: $49 billion USD</strong></p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This budget will be allocated based on different sectors and income support systems as described.</p>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>2. Income Stream from “Thursday Money” (Income Support System):</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The government provides a <strong>$30 weekly income support</strong> to each resident, distributed on Thursdays.</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Population:</strong> 9.5 million people</li>
<li><strong>Weekly payment:</strong> $30 USD per person</li>
<li><strong>Weeks per year:</strong> 52</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">[ \text{Total yearly income support} = 9.5 \text{ million people} \times 30 \text{ USD/week} \times 52 \text{ weeks/year} ]</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">[<br/>\text{Total yearly cost} = 9,500,000 \times 30 \times 52 = 14,820,000,000 \text{ USD}<br/>]</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">So the <strong>total cost for Thursday Money</strong> is <strong>$14.82 billion USD</strong>.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>3. State Employee Salaries:</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">There are <strong>200,000 state employees</strong>, each earning <strong>$2,000 USD per month</strong>, with two bonuses per year, each amounting to <strong>$5,000 USD</strong>.</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Monthly salary per employee:</strong> $2,000</li>
<li><strong>Annual salary per employee (without bonuses):</strong> $2,000 × 12 = $24,000</li>
<li><strong>Bonuses (per year):</strong> 2 bonuses of $5,000 = $10,000</li>
<li><strong>Total annual compensation per employee:</strong> $24,000 + $10,000 = $34,000</li>
<li><strong>Number of state employees:</strong> 200,000</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">[ \text{Total cost for state employee salaries} = 200,000 \times 34,000 = 6,800,000,000 \text{ USD} ]</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">So, the <strong>total yearly cost for state employees</strong> is <strong>$6.8 billion USD</strong>.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>4. Pension for Pensioners:</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Each pensioner receives <strong>$100 USD per week</strong>.</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Number of pensioners</strong> (estimated): 2 million</li>
<li><strong>Weekly pension:</strong> $100 USD</li>
<li><strong>Weeks per year:</strong> 52</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">[<br/>\text{Total yearly pension cost} = 2,000,000 \times 100 \times 52 = 10,400,000,000 \text{ USD}<br/>]</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">So, the <strong>total yearly pension cost</strong> is <strong>$10.4 billion USD</strong>.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>5. Food Card Support:</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Each pensioner receives <strong>$7 USD per day</strong> on a food card.</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Daily food support per pensioner:</strong> $7</li>
<li><strong>Days per year:</strong> 365</li>
<li><strong>Number of pensioners:</strong> 2 million</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">[<br/>\text{Total yearly food card support} = 2,000,000 \times 7 \times 365 = 5,110,000,000 \text{ USD}<br/>]</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">So, the <strong>total yearly cost for food card support</strong> is <strong>$5.11 billion USD</strong>.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>6. Legalized Weed Industry Income for Elderly Women:</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The legal weed industry is controlled by women over the age of 45, providing an income source for this demographic. For simplicity, let’s allocate a <strong>$1 billion USD revenue</strong> generated from this industry as supplemental income for elderly women.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>7. Housing Program:</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Building <strong>20,000 flats per year</strong> through state-supported work programs.</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Average cost per flat:</strong> $100,000 USD</li>
<li><strong>Number of flats:</strong> 20,000</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">[<br/>\text{Total housing program cost} = 20,000 \times 100,000 = 2,000,000,000 \text{ USD}<br/>]</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">So, the <strong>total yearly cost for the housing program</strong> is <strong>$2 billion USD</strong>.</p>

<p class="wp-block-paragraph">A kormány <strong>heti 30 USD</strong> jövedelemtámogatást nyújt minden lakosnak, csütörtökönként.</p>

<li><strong>Évek heteinek száma:</strong> 52</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">[<br/>\text{Teljes évi jövedelemtámogatás} = 9,5 \text{ millió fő} \times 30 \text{ USD/hét} \times 52 \text{ hét/év}<br/>]</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">[<br/>\text{Teljes évi költség} = 9.500.000 \times 30 \times 52 = 14.820.000.000 USD = 5.483.400.000.000 HUF<br/>]</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>3. Állami Alkalmazottak Fizetése:</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Az állami alkalmazottak száma <strong>200.000 fő</strong>, mindegyik havi <strong>2000 USD</strong> fizetést kap, plusz évi két <strong>5000 USD</strong> bónuszt.</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Havi fizetés alkalmazottanként:</strong> 2000 USD</li>
<li><strong>Éves fizetés bónusz nélkül:</strong> 2000 × 12 = 24.000 USD</li>
<li><strong>Éves bónusz:</strong> 2 × 5000 USD = 10.000 USD</li>
<li><strong>Teljes éves jövedelem alkalmazottanként:</strong> 34.000 USD</li>
<li><strong>Alkalmazottak száma:</strong> 200.000</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">[<br/>\text{Teljes költség az állami alkalmazottak fizetésére} = 200.000 \times 34.000 = 6.800.000.000 USD = 2.516.000.000.000 HUF<br/>]</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Tehát az <strong>állami alkalmazottak éves összköltsége</strong> <strong>2.516 milliárd forint</strong>.</p>

<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Tehát a <strong>nyugdíjak éves költsége</strong> <strong>3.848 milliárd forint</strong>.</p>
