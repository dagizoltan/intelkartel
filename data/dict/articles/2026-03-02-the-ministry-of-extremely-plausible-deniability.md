<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><em>Note: The following is fictional satire. It does not describe real events or real institutions.</em></p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">The Ministry of Extremely Plausible Deniability</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">In a small, slightly crooked office wedged between a closed bakery and a permanently “renovating” metro station, the Ministry of Extremely Plausible Deniability ran its recruitment drive.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Their slogan was simple:</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>“Not technically official. Not technically illegal. Not technically anything.”</strong></p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The recruiters introduced themselves as “Former-Adjacent Law Enforcement Consultants.” Their uniforms were convincing from the waist up. Below the belt: gym shorts and sandals.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">“Are you real police?” applicants would ask.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The recruiters would nod gravely.<br>“We used to stand near police.”</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">The Pitch</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The presentation began with a PowerPoint titled:</p>



<blockquote class="wp-block-quote is-layout-flow wp-block-quote-is-layout-flow">
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><em>Totally Voluntary International Adventure Opportunity (Please Don’t Screenshot)</em></p>
</blockquote>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Slide 1:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Competitive pay (in untraceable coupons)</li>



<li>Complimentary mustache</li>



<li>Vague patriotic feelings</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Slide 2:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Location: “Somewhere East-ish”</li>



<li>Objective: “Support Stability Through Mild Confusion”</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Slide 3:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Legal status: “Complicated, but spiritually fine.”</li>
</ul>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Recruitment Strategy</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">They targeted people who:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Had once posted a blurry photo in camouflage.</li>



<li>Used the phrase “back in my day” unironically.</li>



<li>Owned at least one pair of boots they described as “tactical.”</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The recruiters assured everyone:</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">“We are not sending you into a conflict. We are sending you into a <em>dynamic cross-border networking environment.</em>”</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">One applicant raised his hand.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">“Is this about Ukraine?”</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The recruiter coughed loudly and switched slides.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">“This is about personal growth.”</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">The Fake X-Police Division</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The elite branch was called the “X-Police.”<br>No one knew what the X stood for.</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Ex-police?</li>



<li>Extra police?</li>



<li>Experimental police?</li>



<li>Police adjacent but legally interpretive?</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Their badges were impressive.<br>They were also made of laminated bus passes.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Their official jurisdiction extended to:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Parking lots</li>



<li>International misunderstandings</li>



<li>Wherever they happened to be standing confidently</li>
</ul>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Training</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Training included:</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>How to look official while knowing absolutely nothing.</li>



<li>Advanced shrugging under questioning.</li>



<li>The legal phrase:<br>“I am here in a personal capacity.”</li>
</ol>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Graduates received a certificate reading:</p>



<blockquote class="wp-block-quote is-layout-flow wp-block-quote-is-layout-flow">
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">“Certified in Strategic Ambiguity.”</p>
</blockquote>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Plausible Deniability Department</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">If anything went wrong, the Ministry had a prepared statement:</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">“We are shocked. SHOCKED. These individuals were acting independently, possibly while thinking very loudly.”</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The statement was pre-written.<br>It only required filling in the date and the word “regrettable.”</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">The Real Goal</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Nobody was entirely sure what the goal was.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Some said it was geopolitics.<br>Some said it was budget optimization.<br>One intern said it was a group project that got out of hand.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">But deep down, everyone knew:</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The real mission was paperwork.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Because nothing frightens a bureaucracy more than a quiet afternoon.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Epilogue</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Eventually, the entire operation dissolved when someone asked for:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Clear jurisdiction</li>



<li>Written authorization</li>



<li>Or matching socks</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The Ministry quietly rebranded itself as:</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>The Department of Historical Reinterpretation and Occasional Gardening.</strong></p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Recruitment continues every Tuesday.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Please bring your own laminated badge.</p>
