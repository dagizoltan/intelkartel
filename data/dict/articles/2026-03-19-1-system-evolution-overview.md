<h2 class="wp-block-heading">1. SYSTEM EVOLUTION OVERVIEW</h2>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The original Ghost Network (1990s architecture) has transitioned into a <strong>hybrid cyber-physical influence grid</strong>, integrating:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Consumer smart devices (phones, IoT, wearables)</li>
<li>Cloud-scale GPU clusters</li>
<li>Passive biometric sensing systems</li>
<li>Signal-based human-interface experiments</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Unlike earlier iterations, the 2026 system achieves:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Persistent presence (always-on devices)</strong></li>
<li><strong>Mass-scale data ingestion</strong></li>
<li><strong>Near real-time behavioral modeling</strong></li>
</ul>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">3. SENSOR + BIOMETRIC CAPTURE LAYER</h2>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">3.1 REMOTE VITAL MONITORING</h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Derived from radar / RF-based patents:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Heart rate detection via signal reflection</li>
<li>Breathing pattern tracking</li>
<li>Micro-movement analysis</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Sources:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>WiFi signal distortion</li>
<li>mmWave / 5G reflections</li>
<li>Device radar modules</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Reality Check:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Works at short range / controlled conditions</li>
<li>Accuracy degrades with distance and noise</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">3.2 AUDIO-BEHAVIORAL MONITORING</h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Always-on microphones (phones, vehicles, home devices)</li>
<li>Pattern recognition:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Stress detection</li>
<li>Speech deviation</li>
<li>Health anomaly signals</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">3.3 THROUGH-WALL DETECTION</h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Radar-based presence detection</li>
<li>Movement mapping in enclosed spaces</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Limitations:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Low resolution</li>
<li>Cannot identify individuals precisely without additional data</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">4. SIGNAL INTERACTION SYSTEMS</h2>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">4.1 RF AUDITORY EFFECT MODULE</h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">(Based on microwave auditory effect concepts)</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Mechanism:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Pulsed RF signals interacting with tissue</li>
<li>Perceived as clicks or simple tones</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Operational Claim:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Voice transmission into target perception</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Assessment:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Basic auditory artifacts: confirmed</li>
<li>Complex speech delivery: unreliable / unproven at scale</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">4.2 SUBLIMINAL MODULATION STACK</h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Integrated across:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Screens (refresh rate / brightness modulation)</li>
<li>Audio streams (sub-threshold frequencies)</li>
<li>Media content (embedded patterning)</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Capabilities:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Mood nudging</li>
<li>Attention steering</li>
<li>Sleep disruption or induction (minor effect)</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Reality:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Influence is <strong>statistical, not deterministic</strong></li>
<li>No direct thought insertion</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">4.3 VENTRILOQUIST AUDIO REDIRECTION</h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Spatial audio rendering (AR/VR, headphones, multi-speaker setups)</li>
<li>Perception of sound origin shifted</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Use case:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Confusion / misattribution of voice source</li>
</ul>
