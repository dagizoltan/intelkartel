<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Intelligence Report on Ukrainian Master Plan</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">To travel across the world, connect with people, and live peacefully, while also addressing the challenges of:</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Soviet Privatization and Organized Crime</strong>:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Engaging with issues tied to the 4 million Russian-affiliated crime members in the U.S. and the 300 million strategic partners of the 144 million-strong Russian Federation.</li>



<li>Tackling fear instilled by transnational crime networks and moving toward a post-war world governed by the rule of law rather than the law of the jungle.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Transparency and Security</strong>:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Establishing a self-defense pact among law-abiding citizens to counter human trafficking, organ trafficking, drug networks, bribery, and organized criminals exploiting debt-ridden families.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Ukraine’s Master Plan</strong>:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Investigating the privatization of the Soviet Union and countering Russian and Soviet influence globally.</li>



<li>Supporting citizens to migrate, rebuild lives, and seek peace (“Exodus 2025”) following anticipated inauguration and a peace accord.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Post-War Risks</strong>:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Preventing cycles of revenge and addressing threats posed by returning soldiers who may risk harming women and children or becoming associated with criminal activities.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Key Question</strong>:<br>Is Ukraine’s master plan focused on dismantling past Soviet legacies, countering global crime, or providing its citizens with an opportunity to leave and live peacefully elsewhere?</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This report highlights the dual focus on systemic reform and the personal pursuit of peace for Ukrainians in the face of significant geopolitical and social challenges.</p>
