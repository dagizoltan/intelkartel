### **8. Observations / Key Points**

1. **All U.S. military and law enforcement personnel operate under legal, institutional authority**, not political party control.
1. **Private security firms** employ large numbers of personnel but lack sovereign enforcement powers.
1. **Combined U.S. armed, law enforcement, and major security workforce** exceeds 3 million personnel.
1. Hierarchies are well-defined: federal → state → local → contractor management chains.
1. No credible evidence exists of “partisan private armed forces” or paramilitary formations numbering >5,000 under political parties.
