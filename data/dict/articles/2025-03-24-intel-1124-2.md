<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Manifesto of Israel 2: The Runaway State</strong></p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Preamble</strong><br/>We, the children of the forsaken, the abandoned, the institutionalized—those who lost their parents to the streets, to the vices of the past era, to drug dealing and prostitution—declare our intent to forge a new nation. We are the remnants of a world that discarded us, the victims of its excess, its violence, and its indifference. But we refuse to be ghosts in someone else’s history. We shall carve our own destiny. We shall create Israel 2.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Article 1: The Birth of a Nation</strong><br/>Israel 2 shall rise as a sovereign entity in the land of Suriname, a refuge for those born into chaos and abandoned by society. Here, we will no longer be labeled as the children of crime, of shame, of forgotten bloodlines. Instead, we will build a society that embraces us as we are: survivors of a world that sought to erase us.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Article 2: The Right to Exist</strong><br/>We reject the moral condemnations of the old world. We are not here to apologize for our origins. The bloodlines of those once branded as criminals, as outcasts, shall continue through us. Our existence is our defiance. We are the inheritors of resilience, and we shall secure our right to live freely, unjudged and unshackled.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Article 3: Governance and Sovereignty</strong><br/>Israel 2 shall be a self-governed state, ruled by the children of the forsaken. We shall not bow to external influences nor to the morality of those who abandoned us. Our leadership will arise from among us, ensuring that no outsider dictates our fate.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Article 4: Economy and Society</strong><br/>Our state will function outside the conventional global order. We shall establish an economy that supports our people, creating self-sufficiency through innovation, resourcefulness, and mutual aid. While the world sees us as the unwanted, we shall see each other as irreplaceable. We shall not seek approval, only survival and prosperity on our own terms.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Article 5: A Nation Apart</strong><br/>Israel 2 shall not interfere with the affairs of the old world, nor shall we allow its influence to corrupt our purpose. We exist not to threaten, but to live—free from the scorn of those who see us as the mistakes of history. If we are to be forgotten, let it be on our own soil, under our own sky, bound by our own choices.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Conclusion</strong><br/>We are the lost children of a lost generation, but we will not remain lost. We will not ask for a place at the table; we will build our own. We are Israel 2. We are the unwanted, forging a future where we are wanted by each other. And in that, we shall find peace.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><em>Let those who abandoned us keep their world. We shall build our own.</em></p>
