<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Military Strategy Paper: Humanistic Approach to Population Reduction in Eurasia</strong></p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Objective:</strong><br>To reduce the population of Eurasia to 90 million by the year 2150 using a systematic, humanistic approach. This strategy focuses on leveraging controlled structural collapse and drug monopolies for financing, while ensuring a gradual and manageable population decline.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Strategic Plan Overview</strong></p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Current Population Context</strong>:<br>In 2024, the Eurasian population is estimated at 5,000 million (5 billion). The proposed strategy will allow for a controlled decline, targeting a population of 90 million by 2150. The majority of the reduction will occur via natural attrition mechanisms rather than direct intervention.</li>



<li><strong>Population Decline Framework</strong>:</li>
</ol>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Phase One</strong> (2024–2050):<br>By monopolizing the production and distribution of drugs, a healing and relaxing focus is introduced to society. This serves two purposes:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Financing operations through revenue generation.</li>



<li>Reducing stress and increasing dependency, thus indirectly lowering fertility rates. Half of the current population will pass away before reaching 30 due to skirmishes, limited resources, and substance misuse. The remaining population will have an average of one or no children.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li><strong>Phase Two</strong> (2050–2100):<br>Following the collapse of the first generation, the surviving population will naturally decline to approximately 1,250 million (1.25 billion). Reduced fertility, exacerbated by diseases like HPV, will lead to widespread infertility.</li>



<li><strong>Phase Three</strong> (2100–2150):<br>As the population continues to shrink, a healthy remnant of 90 million people will emerge. By this stage, reproductive capabilities will be minimal due to structural and health limitations.</li>
</ul>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Sustainability Measures</strong>:</li>
</ol>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Economic Restructuring</strong>: Post-decline, a sustainable, inheritable economy will emerge. Stable technologies designed for longevity, similar to precision engineering in Patek Philippe watches, will ensure long-term functionality.</li>



<li><strong>Selective Reproduction</strong>: The next generation will be comprised of cloned individuals selected for optimal genetic traits, focusing on both nurture and nature.</li>
</ul>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Outcome</strong><br>The population will stabilize at 90 million by 2150, ensuring a sustainable ratio of resources to population. All individuals will live fulfilling lives, with their existence supported by enduring technologies and efficient resource allocation. A healthy ecosystem of great individuals will lead the world into a new era of prosperity and stability.</p>
