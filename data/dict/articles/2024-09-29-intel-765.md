<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">Here’s an outline and a satirical paper draft that uses dark humor and exaggerated absurdity to critique societal inequalities, corruption, and the war on drugs. The goal is to make the humor ridiculous enough that it serves as a parody of the very concepts it’s exaggerating, without encouraging harm.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Title:</strong> “Operation Nouveau Suppression: A Tactical Guide to Restoring Old Money Supremacy”</h3>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Introduction</strong></h4>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">In a world where the ancient aristocrats struggle to retain their rightful dominance, a terrifying threat looms large: the <em>Nouveau Riche</em>. These self-made entrepreneurs, tech moguls, and lottery winners have dared to encroach upon the sacred space of inherited wealth. The only logical solution? Eliminate the problem at its source: their children. After all, what better way to balance the scales of inequality than by destroying the futures of the young and affluent—using nothing less than military precision?</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This paper outlines a 5-step satirical strategy to save society by reducing the <em>nouveau riche</em> population, ensuring that wealth and privilege remain exactly where they belong: in the hands of the people who’ve had it for centuries.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Step 1: Operation Ivy League Shutdown</strong></h3>



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">Infiltrate the schools. Everyone knows that the offspring of the <em>nouveau riche</em> attend Ivy League universities, intent on achieving their own version of “success.” By embedding operatives as guidance counselors and admissions officers, we can subtly steer them toward less glamorous careers—like reality TV show participation or cryptocurrency investment. Before they know it, they’ll be too busy creating Instagram brands and NFTs to notice the impending doom of their family fortunes.</p>



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph"><em>Satirical Note</em>: The true rich never work for their money; they make their money work for them. The <em>nouveau riche</em> should learn this the hard way—by losing it all in the dog-eat-dog world of digital entrepreneurship.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Step 2: Weaponize Recreational Habits</strong></h3>



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">Let’s admit it: the <em>nouveau riche</em> love a good vice. Parties, private yachts, and Vegas weekends—perfect settings for “Operation Fun-Run.” Military strategists could organize highly exclusive “drug-fueled adventure retreats,” marketing them as exclusive, high-society events. Only this time, the drugs will have a special twist: a cocktail of bad decisions.</p>



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">We’ll make drugs <em>too</em> trendy. Soon, they’ll all be so engrossed in trading psychedelics, they won’t even realize their stock portfolios have tanked, their properties have foreclosed, and the IRS is knocking. Instead of building empires, they’ll be stuck chasing hallucinations and trending hashtags. How ironic, right?</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Step 3: Instagram Infiltration: The Follower War</strong></h3>



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">Social media is the lifeblood of the <em>nouveau riche</em>’s ego. They thrive on likes, shares, and influencer validation. The tactical approach here is simple: create a covert army of bots to follow, unfollow, and spam the accounts of their children with either wildly unearned praise or vitriolic insults.</p>



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">This will lead to digital chaos. Confused and overwhelmed by their rapidly fluctuating follower counts and erratic engagement metrics, these offspring will spiral into existential crises, unable to distinguish between real influence and meaningless clout. Destruction by vanity, one selfie at a time.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Step 4: Elite Boarding School Disinformation Campaign</strong></h3>



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">Next, we must infiltrate elite boarding schools—the breeding grounds of the next generation of <em>nouveau riche</em>. Operatives posing as life coaches will subtly suggest exciting new career paths like “professional YouTuber” or “underground EDM DJ,” guiding these future elites away from politics, law, and finance.</p>



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">Encouraging them to take gap years spent “finding themselves” in Bali will further delay their inevitable inheritance of power. By the time they return, spiritually “enlightened” but financially clueless, the damage will be done. They’ll be too busy manifesting good vibes to notice that their trust funds have mysteriously dwindled.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Step 5: Reverse Trust Fund Engineering</strong></h3>



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">We all know that the secret to controlling wealth is mastering the art of inheritance. So, naturally, we must devise ways to reverse this for the <em>nouveau riche</em>. Using a combination of bureaucratic red tape, tax audits, and “legal advisement,” we can systematically erode these families’ fortunes. Their children’s trust funds will be transformed into “fun funds,” restricted to spending on essential oils, TikTok challenges, and failed attempts to start wellness empires.</p>



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">The children will thank us, believing we’ve liberated them from the chains of their bourgeoisie lineage, while in reality, we’ve simply freed them from any future financial security. The true aristocracy knows better than to rely on family wealth. That’s what offshore accounts and shell companies are for.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Conclusion: The Return of the Old Guard</strong></h3>



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">By executing “Operation Nouveau Suppression” with the precision of a military campaign, we can ensure that the <em>nouveau riche</em>—and more importantly, their progeny—never rise to challenge the established order. In this satirical vision of society, we restore the balance by upholding the time-honored tradition of wealth through bloodlines, land holdings, and generational connections to Swiss bankers. After all, only those who were <em>born rich</em> deserve to <em>stay rich</em>.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Let the children of the <em>nouveau riche</em> spend their lives chasing fleeting fame and doomed investments. The old guard will be waiting, their fortunes intact, as they have been for centuries.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Disclaimer:</strong> <em>This paper is intended as a satirical critique of societal inequalities, wealth, and privilege. It is not a recommendation for real-world actions and should not be interpreted as anything other than a parody of power dynamics.</em></p>

The point is to mock these systems rather than to advocate for harm.</p>
