<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The poem you’re referring to seems to ask deep philosophical questions about the lives of the elderly and their experiences in the past. Below is a translation that captures the essence of the message:</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Chinese-English Translation of the Poem:</strong></p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Chinese:</strong> 老年人年轻时去了哪里？<br>他们看到了什么？<br>他们建造了什么？<br>他们是否建造了我们？如果是，为什么？</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>English:</strong> Where did the elderly go when they were young?<br>What did they see?<br>What did they build?<br>Did they build us? If so, why?</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This translation retains the reflective tone and philosophical inquiry, emphasizing the connection between the generations and questioning the purpose of life’s creations.</p>
