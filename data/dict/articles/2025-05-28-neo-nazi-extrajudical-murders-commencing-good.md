<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>CLASSIFIED</strong><br/><strong>DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE</strong><br/><strong>SITUATION REPORT (SITREP)</strong></p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>From:</strong> Lt. Col. Marcus Reigns, Commanding Officer<br/><strong>To:</strong> Joint Operations Command (JOC) – Security &amp; Intelligence Division<br/><strong>Subject:</strong> Unauthorized Targeted Killings – Far-Right Extremist Elements<br/><strong>Date:</strong> 28 MAY 2025<br/><strong>Time:</strong> 1300 ZULU</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>1. SUMMARY:</strong><br/>Beginning approximately 0800 local time, multiple extrajudicial executions targeting known far-right extremist figures and individuals with neo-Nazi affiliations were reported across multiple urban locations. Attacks occurred in open daylight with high public visibility. Witness accounts confirm the presence of unidentified assailants employing military-grade tactics and weapons.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>2. DETAILS OF INCIDENTS:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Location(s):</strong> Berlin, Warsaw, Vienna, and Budapest</li>
<li><strong>Confirmed Fatalities:</strong> 27 (as of 1200 ZULU)</li>
<li><strong>Suspected Affiliation of Victims:</strong> Documented neo-Nazi organizers, propagandists, and paramilitary leaders</li>
<li><strong>Methods of Execution:</strong> Gunfire (suppressed and unsuppressed), explosives, and close-quarters combat; significant evidence of planned, coordinated strikes.</li>
<li><strong>Public Exposure:</strong> Multiple civilian witnesses; bystander videos circulating on social media platforms</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>3. EYEWITNESS/INTEL REPORTS:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Eyewitnesses report coordinated timing across cities within 15-minute windows.</li>
<li>Assailants reportedly wore nondescript tactical gear, some in masks.</li>
<li>Victims were targeted individually or in small groups; most were killed instantly.</li>
<li>No group has claimed responsibility, though signs suggest anti-fascist militant operations.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>4. INITIAL ASSESSMENT:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Operational Sophistication:</strong> High</li>
<li><strong>Potential Actors:</strong> Currently unknown. Possibilities include rogue paramilitary cells, international vigilante networks, or unacknowledged intelligence operations.</li>
<li><strong>Political Fallout Risk:</strong> Extreme – urgent need for damage control and international coordination.</li>
</ul>
