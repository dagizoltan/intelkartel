<h1 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Declaration of the People’s Senate of Hungary</strong></h1>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><em>Royal Mandate for Cultural Unity and Sonic Renaissance</em></h2>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><em>Electing the King of Hungarian Techno to Harmonize the World</em></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Date:</strong> June 2025<br/><strong>Issued by:</strong> The Hungarian People’s Senate (HPS), Cultural and Rhythmic Affairs Committee</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>I. Preamble:</strong></h2>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">In recognition of Hungary’s ancient spirit, modern ingenuity, and the timeless power of rhythm, the People’s Senate hereby moves to <strong>elect a symbolic monarch</strong> — not of crown and sword, but of sound and light.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This leader shall be known as the <strong>King of Hungarian Techno</strong> — a unifying figure tasked with spreading joy, harmony, and pan-Central European rhythm across the globe. His reign shall last <strong>two years</strong>, and his powers shall be <strong>ceremonial, cultural, and musical</strong>.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>II. Mandate and Mission:</strong></h2>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The King of Hungarian Techno shall:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>🎵 Lead <strong>a global sonic initiative</strong> uniting Hungarian, Romani, Balkan, Slavic, Jewish, and Danubian musical traditions</li>
<li>🌍 Host <strong>Transcontinental Party Waves</strong> — international festivals of unity, featuring artists from all conflict and peace zones</li>
<li>📡 Represent Hungary in <strong>cultural diplomacy missions</strong> from Tokyo to São Paulo, from Lagos to Lviv</li>
<li>🧬 Fund and front <strong>the Hungarian Sonic Genome Project</strong>, preserving and remixing centuries of folk, classical, and underground sound</li>
<li>💽 Release an <strong>Official People’s Album</strong> every 6 months: one track from every ethnic group represented in Hungary</li>
<li>🔥 Open the annual <strong>Budapest Night Summit</strong> with a ritual sunrise set atop the Citadella</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>III. Eligibility and Election:</strong></h2>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Candidates must be:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Born or spiritually adopted into the <strong>Hungarian Cultural Sphere</strong></li>
<li>A recognized producer, DJ, composer, or musical visionary</li>
<li>Endorsed by at least <strong>30 regional music factions</strong> and 100,000 citizen-senators</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Election by:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Full <strong>People’s Senate majority vote</strong></li>
<li>Nationwide digital caucus</li>
<li>Live broadcast with musical performances, public commentary, and AI-aided fairness monitoring</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>IV. Term and Powers:</strong></h2>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Term: <strong>2 years</strong>, non-renewable</li>
<li>Style: <strong>His Sonic Majesty</strong> (or alternative of their choosing)</li>
<li>Powers:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Open and close national events with a set</li>
<li>Grant the <strong>Order of the Rhythm</strong> to cultural icons</li>
<li>Call <strong>National Dance Days</strong> (3x/year), canceling work and uniting citizens in shared joy</li>
<li>Appoint up to 7 <strong>Ambassadors of Bass</strong> for foreign missions</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>V. Legacy and Impact:</strong></h2>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The King’s mission is not entertainment, but <strong>collective spiritual and cultural elevation</strong>. Through rhythm, we remember who we are. Through harmony, we overcome difference. Through joy, we become a light among nations.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Let this monarchy be symbolic. Let the crown be a subwoofer. Let the sceptre pulse at 128 BPM.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>VI. Conclusion:</strong></h2>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Hungary shall not only rise through economics and defense, but through <strong>culture, sound, and soul</strong>.<br/>Let the Senate now prepare for the <strong>Royal Techno Coronation of 2026</strong>.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Long Live the Beat. Long Live the King. Long Live Hungary.</strong></p>
