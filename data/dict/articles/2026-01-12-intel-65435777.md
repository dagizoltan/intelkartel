<h3 class="wp-block-heading">IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This document <strong>does not confirm facts</strong>. It consolidates <strong>claims and allegations only</strong>, presented in a severe tone due to their potential implications. Treat as an <strong>early-warning document</strong>, not a finding of guilt.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Below is a <strong>strict, professional EVIDENCE CHECKLIST</strong> used by <strong>real investigators, prosecutors, and intelligence analysts</strong>.<br>If evidence does <strong>not</strong> meet these standards, the claim <strong>cannot be treated as factual</strong>, regardless of how serious it sounds.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This checklist is intentionally <strong>hard</strong>. That is how credibility is established.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h1 class="wp-block-heading">EVIDENCE CHECKLIST</h1>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>For Allegations of Foreign Intelligence, Paramilitary, or Weaponized Harassment</strong></p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">1. SOURCE &amp; TESTIMONY VALIDATION</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">☐ <strong>Named witnesses</strong> (real identities known to investigators)<br>☐ <strong>Sworn statements</strong> or affidavits<br>☐ <strong>Multiple independent witnesses</strong> with no coordination<br>☐ Consistent timelines across testimonies<br>☐ No financial, political, or personal incentive detected<br>☐ Witness mental health evaluations (to rule out stress-induced misattribution)</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">⚠️ Anonymous or online-only accounts = <strong>not evidence</strong>, only leads</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">2. DOCUMENTARY EVIDENCE</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">☐ Official documents (court records, visas, contracts, payrolls)<br>☐ Immigration or border-entry records<br>☐ Property leases or business registrations tied to suspects<br>☐ Communications metadata (emails, phone logs—not screenshots alone)<br>☐ Financial records showing funding or payments</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">⚠️ Claims without documents = <strong>allegations only</strong></p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">3. DIGITAL &amp; TECHNICAL FORENSICS</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">☐ Devices seized and examined by <strong>certified forensic labs</strong><br>☐ Verifiable malware or surveillance implants detected<br>☐ Network traffic logs showing external control or monitoring<br>☐ Hardware confirmed to perform claimed functions<br>☐ Chain of custody preserved for all devices</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">⚠️ Feelings of monitoring ≠ proof of monitoring</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">4. MEDICAL &amp; PHYSICAL EVIDENCE</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><em>(Critical for weapon or harassment claims)</em></p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">☐ Medical records from licensed hospitals<br>☐ Diagnostic imaging (MRI, CT, EEG)<br>☐ Toxicology and blood panels<br>☐ Independent evaluations by <strong>multiple specialists</strong><br>☐ Findings consistent with <strong>known, documented technologies</strong></p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">⚠️ No recognized medical signature = no proof of weapon use</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">5. WEAPONS &amp; TECHNOLOGY CLAIMS</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">☐ Physical device recovered<br>☐ Manufacturer identified<br>☐ Technology exists in <strong>peer-reviewed literature</strong><br>☐ Capabilities demonstrated under controlled conditions<br>☐ Effects reproducible by independent experts</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">⚠️ “Secret,” “undetectable,” or “experimental” weapons<br>→ <strong>Extraordinary claims require extraordinary proof</strong></p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">6. GEOLOCATION &amp; MOVEMENT DATA</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">☐ Verified GPS or telecom location records<br>☐ CCTV footage with timestamps<br>☐ Travel records (airlines, hotels, border control)<br>☐ Correlation between suspect presence and alleged incidents</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">⚠️ Large-scale presence claims without movement data are invalid</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">7. LAW ENFORCEMENT &amp; OFFICIAL RECORDS</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">☐ Police reports filed at time of incidents<br>☐ Prosecutorial case numbers<br>☐ Arrests, seizures, or interrogations<br>☐ Court proceedings or warrants<br>☐ Requests for mutual legal assistance (MLA)</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">⚠️ No official engagement = no institutional confirmation</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">8. INTERNATIONAL VERIFICATION</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">☐ UN Special Rapporteur inquiries<br>☐ EU or OSCE monitoring reports<br>☐ NATO or diplomatic confirmations<br>☐ Statements from neutral third-party states<br>☐ NGO investigations (Amnesty, HRW, etc.)</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">⚠️ Social media amplification ≠ international validation</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">9. NUMERICAL &amp; SCALE CLAIMS</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">☐ Population statistics from official registries<br>☐ Visa and residency data<br>☐ Employment and tax records<br>☐ Housing and infrastructure capacity analysis</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">⚠️ Large numbers without data = <strong>automatically rejected</strong></p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">10. DISINFORMATION &amp; PSYOPS SCREENING</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><em>(Mandatory step)</em></p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">☐ Narrative overlap with known influence campaigns<br>☐ Language markers of amplification networks<br>☐ Cross-posting patterns<br>☐ Emotional escalation indicators<br>☐ Verification against known misinformation databases</p>
