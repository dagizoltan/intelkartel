<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">When a police officer, soldier, or operative is fatally wounded in situations involving urgent exits, rogue nations, kill squads, or other high-risk scenarios such as judge-jury-executioner programs, hitman programs, military intelligence academies, or private military contractors, providing a <strong>last dying testament</strong> to describe the events that led to their fatal injury is crucial. This testament can serve as an important legal or operational record and may provide critical intelligence or evidence to prevent further harm or pursue justice.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Here’s a <strong>general protocol</strong> for facilitating a last dying testament under these intense and dangerous circumstances:</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>1. Ensure Immediate Security</strong></h3>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Situation Awareness:</strong> First and foremost, ensure that the environment is secure enough to allow the individual to speak. Quickly move the officer/operative to a safe location if possible, or ensure they are not exposed to immediate threats. Secure the area or ask for immediate backup.</li>



<li><strong>Assess Medical Care:</strong> While recording the testament is vital, do everything you can to provide medical care to save their life. Stabilization should be the first priority if feasible.</li>
</ul>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>2. Begin the Recording or Testimony</strong></h3>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Record the Statement</strong>: If the individual is conscious and able to communicate, <strong>immediately begin recording</strong> using any available device (phone, body camera, audio recorder). This serves as both legal documentation and a means to capture any intelligence.</li>



<li>If recording is impossible, write down their words <strong>verbatim</strong> and make sure another witness is present to corroborate the statement if possible.</li>



<li>Be clear with the officer that this is <strong>their last opportunity to give an official statement</strong>, ensuring that they understand the importance.</li>
</ul>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>3. Ask Key Questions Promptly</strong></h3>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Time is of the essence. Begin by asking <strong>direct, concise questions</strong> that focus on the critical information. Key questions might include:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Who attacked you?</strong></li>



<li><strong>What happened?</strong></li>



<li><strong>Where did the attack occur?</strong></li>



<li><strong>What forces were involved?</strong> (rogue nations, hit squads, military contractors, etc.)</li>



<li><strong>What are the next imminent threats or risks?</strong></li>



<li><strong>Who needs to be warned or alerted?</strong></li>



<li><strong>Are there any internal collaborators, rogue elements, or compromised individuals?</strong></li>



<li><strong>What mission or operation were you engaged in?</strong></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>4. Gather Tactical Information</strong></h3>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Request specifics</strong> on the nature of the threat:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Names or descriptions of attackers, rogue units, or contractors.</li>



<li>Details about the nature of the mission or operation that led to the ambush.</li>



<li>Tactical details such as the number of assailants, weapons used, and any notable uniforms, insignia, or identifying marks.</li>



<li>Any information on compromised security or infiltration that could prevent further casualties.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>5. Offer the Opportunity to Identify Further Threats</strong></h3>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>If the officer has relevant knowledge of ongoing operations or potential risks, ask for any information that could prevent further attacks or exposure of others. For instance:<ul><li><strong>“Is there anything we need to know to stop them?”</strong></li><li><strong>“What should we be looking for next?”</strong></li><li><strong>“Are there others at risk or any vulnerabilities?”</strong></li></ul>This could be information about impending attacks, compromised assets, hidden locations, or rogue elements within the organization.</li>
</ul>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>6. Offer Final Testimony on Personal Wishes or Last Words</strong></h3>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>If the officer wishes to give any personal last words or identify final wishes to family, colleagues, or others, this is the moment to allow them to speak freely. Even in professional or military contexts, individuals may wish to:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Leave final words for their loved ones.</li>



<li>Express their commitment to the mission or their values.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>7. Document for Chain of Command</strong></h3>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>After gathering the statement, <strong>immediately pass it up the chain of command</strong>, ensuring it gets to relevant authorities, commanders, or law enforcement. It may contain critical operational or intelligence details that need swift action.</li>



<li>Preserve the evidence carefully. Recordings, written notes, and corroborating witnesses should be protected as legal and operational documents.</li>
</ul>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>8. Honor and Respect</strong></h3>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Throughout the process, maintain a compassionate and respectful demeanor. The officer is likely in immense pain and under intense emotional strain. Provide comfort where possible, but keep the focus on ensuring their testament is clear and actionable.</li>



<li>Acknowledge their sacrifice and reassure them that their testimony will be acted upon.</li>
</ul>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>9. Follow Up With Immediate Investigation and Action</strong></h3>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>After the dying testament is collected, ensure that the <strong>relevant team or agency</strong> acts swiftly to investigate the circumstances and take any action necessary to safeguard others, address the threats, and secure justice.</li>



<li>If the individual mentioned <strong>rogue elements</strong>, <strong>kill squads</strong>, or <strong>compromised actors</strong> within the chain of command, make sure those claims are handled discreetly and securely to prevent further risks.</li>
</ul>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">This protocol balances the urgency of collecting actionable intelligence or evidence while honoring the individual’s service and sacrifice. The dying testament may contain vital details that could save lives, provide justice, or prevent further criminal actions from rogue actors, military contractors, or covert programs.</p>
