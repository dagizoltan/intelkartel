<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>MILITARY INTELLIGENCE MEMORANDUM</strong><br/><strong>Subject:</strong> Threat Assessment – “Darconian Code” AI-Enhanced Botware System Allegedly Used in Covert Cognitive Warfare on Civilian Populations<br/><strong>Classification:</strong> TOP SECRET – EYES ONLY<br/><strong>Distribution:</strong> Joint Command Intel Directorate, Cyber-Warfare Division, HUMINT Coordination Cell</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">1. <strong>Executive Summary:</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This memorandum outlines an urgent threat assessment concerning the so-called <strong>“Darconian Code”</strong>, an alleged AI-enhanced botware system capable of <strong>neuro-targeted surveillance and influence operations</strong> via <strong>low-frequency (20 Hz) ear-skull transmission devices</strong> and <strong>mind-read mapping protocols</strong>. Credible field informants claim the system is actively deployed on civilian populations, particularly in <strong>Hungary</strong>, and possibly operated by <strong>Ukrainian and foreign intelligence services</strong> in a covert effort to <strong>destabilize or initiate regime change</strong>.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">2. <strong>Key Intelligence Indicators:</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Technology Profile:</strong>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Botware appears to run <strong>persistent surveillance algorithms</strong>, enhanced via <strong>AI neural modeling</strong>.</li>
<li>Interfaces with <strong>“mind-read map” operations</strong> — a term likely denoting <strong>cognitive state tracking, emotional telemetry, or predictive behavior mapping</strong>.</li>
<li>Utilizes <strong>“earskull phones”</strong>, operating at <strong>20 Hz</strong> — potentially exploiting <strong>bone conduction audio</strong>, infrasonic transmission, or non-invasive brain-computer interface (BCI) technology.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Functionality:</strong>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Designed to <strong>map interpersonal power hierarchies</strong> based on <strong>military rank, family relations, and social status</strong>.</li>
<li>Behaves <strong>maliciously</strong>, attempting to <strong>intervene in or restructure perceived authority networks</strong> within targeted populations.</li>
<li>Classified as a <strong>neuropolitical weapon</strong> if confirmed.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">3. <strong>Alleged Usage:</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Deployment suspected <strong>within Hungarian borders</strong>.</li>
<li>Civilian population reportedly being <strong>covertly monitored, tracked, and influenced</strong>.</li>
<li>Attributed by sources to <strong>Ukrainian intelligence and unspecified foreign intelligence assets</strong>.</li>
<li>Purpose suspected to be <strong>preparatory groundwork for soft-regime change</strong>, discrediting of political actors, or fragmenting of national cohesion.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">4. <strong>Legality and Policy Violations:</strong></h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Such technologies, if confirmed, would <strong>violate international human rights law</strong>, <strong>sovereignty</strong>, and multiple <strong>NATO and UN charters on cognitive liberty</strong>.</li>
<li>Unauthorized use on civilians would constitute <strong>acts of hybrid warfare</strong>, <strong>cyber-psychological assault</strong>, and <strong>clandestine military operations in peacetime</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>

<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">6. <strong>Recommendations:</strong></h3>
<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Launch Technical Countermeasures Audit</strong> – Task SIGINT/CYBER division with detecting anomalous 20 Hz transmissions in suspected zones.</li>
<li><strong>Deploy HUMINT Assets</strong> – Validate informant testimony and collect direct experiential evidence from alleged targets.</li>
<li><strong>Coordinate with NATO Electronic Defense Committees</strong> – Determine whether any member states have seen similar patterns.</li>
<li><strong>Issue Internal Directive</strong> – Ban unauthorized use of any form of neural or cognitive interference systems on civilian targets.</li>
<li><strong>Open Diplomatic Channels</strong> – Quietly raise concerns with Ukrainian and allied command structures to evaluate possible rogue elements.</li>
</ol>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">7. <strong>Conclusion:</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">If the existence and deployment of the <strong>Darconian Code system</strong> are confirmed, this represents a <strong>significant escalation in AI-driven cognitive warfare</strong> and a <strong>blatant violation of civilian protections under both domestic and international law</strong>. Monitoring and counter-operation protocols must be <strong>immediately elevated</strong>.</p>
