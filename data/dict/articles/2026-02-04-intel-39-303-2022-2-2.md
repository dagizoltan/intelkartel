<h2 class="wp-block-heading">COUNTER‑INTELLIGENCE DECISION TREE</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Use Case:</strong> Insider compromise, telecom / cyber access abuse<br><strong>Goal:</strong> Rapid triage → containment → attribution → disruption</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">START NODE</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Trigger Event Detected</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Unauthorized access</li>



<li>Malware installation</li>



<li>Unlogged configuration change</li>



<li>Telecom routing anomaly</li>



<li>Insider named in external reporting</li>
</ul>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">NODE 1 — IS THERE VERIFIED ACCESS MISUSE?</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Question:</strong><br>Do logs, forensics, or audits confirm <em>actual</em> misuse of credentials or systems?</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>NO</strong> →<br>➤ Treat as <strong>signal noise / false positive</strong><br>➤ Continue monitoring<br>➤ Do <strong>not</strong> initiate personnel action</li>



<li><strong>YES</strong> →<br>➤ Preserve logs<br>➤ Freeze credentials<br>➤ Proceed to Node 2</li>
</ul>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">NODE 2 — IS THE ACT REPEATABLE OR SINGULAR?</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Question:</strong><br>Has the behavior occurred more than once, or across systems?</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>SINGULAR EVENT</strong> →<br>➤ Possible error or coercion entry point<br>➤ Interview under <strong>non‑accusatory CI protocol</strong><br>➤ Proceed to Node 3</li>



<li><strong>REPEATABLE / PATTERNED</strong> →<br>➤ High probability of external influence<br>➤ Activate CI lead<br>➤ Proceed to Node 3</li>



<li></li>
</ul>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">NODE 3 — ACCESS OR IDEOLOGY?</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Critical Gate (Most Failures Occur Here)</strong></p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Question:</strong><br>Is there <em>any</em> verified ideological, political, or group‑based motivation?</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>NO EVIDENCE</strong> →<br>➤ Classify as <strong>Access‑Driven Compromise</strong><br>➤ Proceed to Node 4</li>



<li><strong>CLAIMS / RUMORS ONLY</strong> →<br>➤ Treat as <strong>misattribution risk</strong><br>➤ Ignore for operational purposes<br>➤ Proceed to Node 4</li>



<li><strong>DOCUMENTED EVIDENCE</strong> (rare) →<br>➤ Validate independently<br>➤ Proceed cautiously to Node 4</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">⬇️</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">NODE 4 — LEVERAGE INDICATORS PRESENT?</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Check for any of the following (one is sufficient):</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Undeclared income or debt</li>



<li>Prior policy violations</li>



<li>Credential misuse history</li>



<li>Contractor / third‑party dependency</li>



<li>Recent discipline or termination notice</li>



<li><strong>YES</strong> →<br>➤ High confidence <strong>coercion or brokerage</strong><br>➤ Proceed to Node 5</li>



<li><strong>NO</strong> →<br>➤ Consider negligence or weak controls<br>➤ Still proceed to Node 5 (absence ≠ innocence)</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">⬇️</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">NODE 5 — IS THE SUBJECT A RECRUIT OR A BROKER?</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Question:</strong><br>Does the subject only provide access, or do they recruit / direct others?</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>ACCESS ONLY</strong> →<br>➤ Classified as <strong>Compromised Insider</strong><br>➤ Priority: extraction + containment<br>➤ Proceed to Node 6A</li>



<li><strong>RECRUITS / COORDINATES</strong> →<br>➤ Classified as <strong>Access Broker</strong><br>➤ Priority: network mapping<br>➤ Proceed to Node 6B</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">⬇️</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h2 class="wp-block-heading">NODE 6A — COMPROMISED INSIDER RESPONSE</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Immediate Actions</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Remove access silently</li>



<li>Preserve communications</li>



<li>Offer protected disclosure pathway</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Objective</strong></p>

<li>Break leverage</li>

</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">⬇️</p>
