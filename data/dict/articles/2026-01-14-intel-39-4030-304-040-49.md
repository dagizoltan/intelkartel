<li>The source alleges that certain NGOs and associated entities are involved in <strong>urban warfare techniques</strong> directed against families, using what the source describes as <strong>intolerant family-destruction mechanisms</strong> and activities “against mankind.”</li>



<li>The source claims that these groups were aligned with what is described as the <strong>“gay lobby of America,”</strong> and asserts that these groups are now under significant pressure due to alleged harm caused to family structures.</li>



<li>According to the source, these networks relied heavily on <strong>Obama-era support systems</strong>, which the source asserts were influenced by communist ideology and terrorism. The source claims that materials allegedly read or promoted by Barack Obama influenced these networks.</li>



<li>The source alleges that Barack Obama is a <strong>central figure (“kingpin”)</strong> in an FBI investigation related to the disappearance of children.</li>



<li>The source cites figures claiming:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Approximately <strong>400,000 children disappear annually</strong> from orphanages in the United States.</li>



<li>Approximately <strong>3.2 million children</strong> are allegedly involved in intelligence and NGO operations that the source claims failed America and failed children.</li>
</ul>
</li>



<li>The source alleges that these operations resulted in severe harm to children, describing outcomes as “turning them into mush,” and claims that <strong>substantial evidence</strong> is now in federal custody.</li>



<li>The source further alleges that issues affecting children include <strong>organ harvesting</strong>, among other abuses.</li>
</ol>
