<h2 class="wp-block-heading">1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Nuuk is the political, administrative, economic, and military center of Greenland. It hosts the majority of Greenland’s population and infrastructure, functions as Denmark’s primary Arctic administrative hub, and is strategically located near the North Atlantic–Arctic transition zone. While small by global standards, Nuuk is critical for Arctic governance, logistics, and regional security.</p>
