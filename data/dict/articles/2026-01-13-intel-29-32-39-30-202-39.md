<h2 class="wp-block-heading">MILITARY INTELLIGENCE NOTE</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Subject:</strong> Telecommunications Manipulation &amp; Illegal Call Redirection<br><strong>Area:</strong> Budapest (Urban Telecom Environment)<br><strong>Classification:</strong> SITUATIONAL AWARENESS / DEFENSIVE ACTION REQUIRED<br><strong>Date:</strong> Immediate Relevance</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Multiple reports indicate <strong>systematic redirection and interception of mobile phone communications</strong> in Budapest. Tactics allegedly involve <strong>corrupt law-enforcement elements</strong> cooperating with <strong>criminal networks</strong>, including individuals <strong>posing as investigators or former officers</strong> from previous systems. These actors reportedly exploit <strong>call-forwarding functions</strong>, carrier access, and social engineering to monitor targets without consent.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This note serves as a <strong>call to action</strong> for immediate defensive countermeasures.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">2. THREAT OVERVIEW (REPORTED INDICATORS)</h3>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Unauthorized call forwarding</strong> activated without user knowledge</li>



<li><strong>Silent call redirection</strong> to third-party numbers</li>



<li><strong>Impersonation of detectives / investigators</strong> to intimidate or normalize surveillance</li>



<li><strong>Abuse of legacy relationships</strong> within police or telecom infrastructure</li>



<li><strong>Illegal listening / metadata harvesting</strong> targeting civilians, journalists, business owners, and foreign nationals</li>
</ul>



<blockquote class="wp-block-quote is-layout-flow wp-block-quote-is-layout-flow">
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Assessment: These actions, if occurring, represent <strong>criminal abuse of authority</strong> and <strong>serious violations of privacy and communications law</strong>.</p>
</blockquote>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">3. KEY VULNERABILITY: CALL FORWARDING ABUSE</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Call forwarding can be activated remotely or through deceptive means, resulting in:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Calls being <strong>diverted before reaching the user</strong></li>



<li>Partial interception without obvious signs</li>



<li>Increased exposure to <strong>monitoring and manipulation</strong></li>
</ul>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">4. IMMEDIATE COUNTERMEASURE — CRITICAL</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>ALL USERS SHOULD EXECUTE THE FOLLOWING NOW:</strong></p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">🔴 <strong>Dial:</strong> <code>##002#</code></h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Function:</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Instantly cancels ALL call forwarding</strong></li>



<li>Disables unconditional, conditional, and hidden redirects</li>



<li>Works on most GSM networks (Android &amp; iPhone)</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>This is the fastest and most effective first defense.</strong></p>



<blockquote class="wp-block-quote is-layout-flow wp-block-quote-is-layout-flow">
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Repeat regularly</strong>, especially after:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Interactions with unknown “officials”</li>



<li>Phone service disruptions</li>



<li>SIM changes or roaming</li>



<li>Device servicing or inspections</li>
</ul>
</blockquote>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">5. ADDITIONAL DEFENSIVE ACTIONS</h3>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Check call forwarding status manually in phone settings</li>



<li>Use <strong>end-to-end encrypted messaging</strong> for sensitive communications</li>



<li>Avoid discussing sensitive matters on standard voice calls</li>



<li>Document unusual calls, warnings, or impersonation attempts</li>



<li>Do <strong>not</strong> comply with requests made outside formal, verifiable legal channels</li>
</ul>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">6. CALL TO ACTION</h3>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Assume communications may be compromised until verified secure.</strong><br><strong>Disable call forwarding immediately.</strong><br><strong>Share this defensive measure widely.</strong></p>



<blockquote class="wp-block-quote is-layout-flow wp-block-quote-is-layout-flow">
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Awareness is protection.<br>Silence enables abuse.</p>
</blockquote>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>EXECUTE <code>##002#</code> — NOW.</strong></p>
