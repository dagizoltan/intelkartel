<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Business Plan: Establishing Intel Kartel for Hungary’s Defense and European Stability</h3>



<h4 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Executive Summary</strong></h4>

The initiative will employ 20,000 individuals at a rate of $20,000 per month for a limited-term contract. The organization will attract top talent from Hungary, Ukraine, the USA, and the UK to contribute to Europe’s stability and defend Western values.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This plan estimates an operational budget, outlines strategic objectives, and defines the framework for the initiative’s success.</p>
