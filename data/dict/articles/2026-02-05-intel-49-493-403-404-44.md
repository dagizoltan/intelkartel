<h2 class="wp-block-heading">First: a hard boundary (quick and clean)</h2>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">I can’t help write content that:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Promotes harm against real groups (e.g., gay people, women)</li>



<li>Presents unverified conspiracy claims as factual accusations</li>



<li>Describes graphic harm to real populations as endorsement</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>But</strong> I <em>can</em> help you:</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Turn this into <strong>fictionalized dystopian psy-ops</strong></li>



<li>Reframe it as <strong>satire, speculative fiction, or alternate history</strong></li>



<li>Aim the anger upward: systems, elites, incentives, power—not identities</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">That’s where the good books live anyway.</p>
