<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Analytical Brief: Transport Timeline Calculation</h2>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Subject:</strong> Estimated time required to move 2.1 million personnel by air under varying daily transport rates.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Purpose:</strong><br/>To estimate the number of transport cycles (“waves”) and the total time required to relocate <strong>2,100,000 personnel</strong> assuming a daily airlift capacity ranging from <strong>6,000 to 30,000 individuals per day</strong>.</p>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Key Assumptions</h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Total personnel to transport: <strong>2,100,000</strong></li>
<li>Daily transport throughput ranges from:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Slow pace:</strong> 6,000 individuals/day</li>
<li><strong>Moderate pace:</strong> 15,000 individuals/day</li>
<li><strong>Maximum pace:</strong> 30,000 individuals/day</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>Each “wave” represents <strong>one day of transport operations</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Estimated Waves Required</h3>
<figure class="wp-block-table"><table class="has-fixed-layout"><thead><tr><th>Transport Pace</th><th>Personnel per Wave (Day)</th><th>Waves Required</th><th>Approximate Duration</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>Slow</td><td>6,000</td><td>350 waves</td><td>~350 days</td></tr><tr><td>Moderate</td><td>15,000</td><td>140 waves</td><td>~140 days</td></tr><tr><td>Maximum</td><td>30,000</td><td>70 waves</td><td>~70 days</td></tr></tbody></table></figure>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Observations</h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Increasing daily throughput significantly reduces total duration.</li>
<li>Moving from <strong>6,000/day to 30,000/day</strong> reduces the timeline from <strong>~350 days to ~70 days</strong>.</li>
<li>Even at the highest rate, the movement would require <strong>multiple months of sustained transport activity</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Summary</h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Transporting <strong>2.1 million personnel</strong> by air would require:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>70 waves</strong> at maximum throughput (30,000/day)</li>
<li><strong>140 waves</strong> at moderate throughput (15,000/day)</li>
<li><strong>350 waves</strong> at slow throughput (6,000/day)</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Estimated total timeline ranges from <strong>~70 days to ~350 days</strong>, depending on operational capacity.</p>
