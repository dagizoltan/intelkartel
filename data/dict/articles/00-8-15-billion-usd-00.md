<p class="wp-block-paragraph">1% OF THE PENTAGON BUDGET IS ENOUGH TO BUILD A 7 LINE DEFENSE AS THE DEFENSIVE LINE FROM SWEEDEN TO HUNGARY ALL THE WAY TO ROMANIA.</p>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Summary:</h3>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Number of bunkers</strong>: 40,000</li>



<li><strong>Total cost</strong>: 4 billion USD</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph">So, it would cost approximately <strong>4 billion USD</strong> to build 40,000 bunkers, spaced every 50 meters, along a route from Sweden to Romania.</p>
