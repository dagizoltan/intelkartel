<p class="wp-block-paragraph">WHAT THE FUCK IS THIS WEBSITE? &gt; </p>
<h1 class="wp-block-heading">IntelKartel.com: Content on Strategy and Crime Prevention</h1>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Overview of Content:</strong> IntelKartel is a self-described “dark humor” strategic-intel blog run by Dániel Vidos (a.k.a. “VD”) (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/core-doksi/#:~:text=THIS%20WEBSITE%20IS%20DARK%20HUMOR,WEBSITE%20ABOUT%20THINGS%20THAT%20MAKE">CORE DOKSI – INTEL KARTEL</a>) (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/lobby-proposal-to-prime-minister-orban-viktor-and-the-hungarian-people-3.pdf#:~:text=Vidos%20D%C3%A1niel%20vagyok%2C%20az%20IntelKartel,t%C3%B6r%C3%B6k%2C%20hogy%20megv%C3%A1ltsam%20a%20vil%C3%A1got">Lobby Proposal to Prime Minister Orbán Viktor and the Hungarian People</a>). It mixes satirical and serious intelligence-style reports. Topics range from war planning and national security to domestic policy and crime-prevention schemes. Most content is fictionalized or speculative (often in English), though some posts are in Hungarian. For example, the site explicitly states <em>“THIS WEBSITE IS [a] DARK HUMOR WEBSITE”</em> (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/core-doksi/#:~:text=THIS%20WEBSITE%20IS%20DARK%20HUMOR,WEBSITE%20ABOUT%20THINGS%20THAT%20MAKE">CORE DOKSI – INTEL KARTEL</a>), signaling that its “briefings” are not official intelligence.</li>
<li><strong>Key Published Proposals:</strong> The site publishes numerous policy proposals and analyses. Highlights include:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Organized Crime (Hungary)</strong>: A “Strategy to Combat Organized Crime” outlines strict anti-intimidation laws, dedicated law-enforcement units, witness protection, public hotlines, special courts, asset forfeiture, etc. (e.g. <em>“0. Intolerance for intimidation,” specialized task forces, fast-track trials, and asset seizures</em> (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/#:~:text=,Strengthening%20Penalties">INTEL KARTEL – BRIEF INTEL BRIEFINGS</a>) (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/#:~:text=,Organized%20Crime">INTEL KARTEL – BRIEF INTEL BRIEFINGS</a>)).</li>
<li><strong>Child Safety Scheme (Patent)</strong>: A pseudo-patent for a “crowd-enabled child tracking” system would equip children with GPS/biometric wearables, create a public blockchain database of child offenders, and enable community monitoring to “flag anomalies” (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/#:~:text=">INTEL KARTEL – BRIEF INTEL BRIEFINGS</a>). This extreme surveillance concept is posed as anti-trafficking technology.</li>
<li><strong>Humanitarian/Warfare Scenarios</strong>: The blog includes elaborate scenario-analyses. One imagines genocide of Roma at the Ukraine–Hungary border and recommends invoking UN “R2P”, deploying NATO forces to create safe zones, and shared intel to stop atrocities (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/#:~:text=1,member%20and%20Ukraine%E2%80%99s%20security%20dependencies">INTEL KARTEL – BRIEF INTEL BRIEFINGS</a>) (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/#:~:text=Conclusion%20%26%20Recommendations%3A%20Given%20the,destabilization%20and%20a%20humanitarian%20catastrophe">INTEL KARTEL – BRIEF INTEL BRIEFINGS</a>). These reports read like military whitepapers, calling for immediate multinational action under international law.</li>
<li><strong>Political/Social Programs (Hungary)</strong>: Under Vidos’s political views (“VD Program”), bold social policies are outlined: universal housing (even offering adolescents their own apartment) and a guaranteed weekly cash stipend (10,000 HUF each Thursday to every citizen) (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/magyar-politikai-nezetek/#:~:text=CSUTORTOK%20PENZT%21">DANI (VD) POLITIKAI NEZETEI – INTEL KARTEL</a>) (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/lobby-proposal-to-prime-minister-orban-viktor-and-the-hungarian-people-3.pdf#:~:text=%C3%A9lhet%20%E2%80%93%20a%20lak%C3%A1s%20az,a%20h%C3%A9tv%C3%A9g%C3%A9t%20is%20f%C3%BCggetlen%C3%BCl%20%C3%A9lvezhesse">Lobby Proposal to Prime Minister Orbán Viktor and the Hungarian People</a>). The plan also includes legalizing cannabis in state-run “monopolies” (e.g. by elder cooperatives) (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/vd-program-hungary/#:~:text=DEMOCRATIC%20DEMANDS%20ON%20GOVERNMENT%20FROM,YOTUH%20AND%20ELDERLY">– VD PROGRAM HUNGARY – INTEL KARTEL</a>), and replacing prison with house-arrest or “intelligent quarantine” for criminals (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/magyar-politikai-nezetek/#:~:text=MAX%20BUNTI%3A%20HAZI%20ORIZET%20ES,INTELIGENS%20KARANTEN">DANI (VD) POLITIKAI NEZETEI – INTEL KARTEL</a>). Content is partly in Hungarian (e.g. the slogan <em>“Csütörtök pénzt!”</em> – “Thursday money!” (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/magyar-politikai-nezetek/#:~:text=CSUTORTOK%20PENZT%21">DANI (VD) POLITIKAI NEZETEI – INTEL KARTEL</a>)). Another “lobby proposal” to PM Orbán urges spending $90B on bunkers (“240,000 modern bunker-apartments” along the borders) and funding it by auctioning 30,000 passports at $3 M each (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/lobby-proposal-to-prime-minister-orban-viktor-and-the-hungarian-people-3.pdf#:~:text=1.%20Hat%C3%A1rv%C3%A9delem%20meger%C5%91s%C3%ADt%C3%A9se%3A%20A%20Pann%C3%B3niai,modern%20lak%C3%B3terekkel%20%C3%A9s%20integr%C3%A1lt%20v%C3%A9delmi">Lobby Proposal to Prime Minister Orbán Viktor and the Hungarian People</a>) (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/lobby-proposal-to-prime-minister-orban-viktor-and-the-hungarian-people-3.pdf#:~:text=%E2%97%8F%20Ad%C3%B3kedvezm%C3%A9nyek%20az%20IKEA%20%C3%A9s,vez%C3%A9relt%20j%C3%B6v%C5%91re%2C%20amely%20%C3%B6tv%C3%B6zi%20a">Lobby Proposal to Prime Minister Orbán Viktor and the Hungarian People</a>), which would also finance a “universal digital income” of 50 000 HUF weekly per citizen (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/lobby-proposal-to-prime-minister-orban-viktor-and-the-hungarian-people-3.pdf#:~:text=kik%C3%A9pz%C3%A9s,vez%C3%A9relt%20j%C3%B6v%C5%91re%2C%20amely%20%C3%B6tv%C3%B6zi%20a">Lobby Proposal to Prime Minister Orbán Viktor and the Hungarian People</a>).</li>
<li><strong>Satirical/Conspiratorial “Reports”:</strong> The site also carries bizarre conspiratorial papers. One example claims left-wing extremists are training orphan-hit-squads to kill elderly citizens for their property (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/#:~:text=Title%3A%20The%20Weaponization%20of%20Orphan,for%20Financial%20and%20Ideological%20Gain">INTEL KARTEL – BRIEF INTEL BRIEFINGS</a>). Another brief warns of a global “orphan network” pushing an “Everydayism” ideology and recommends infiltrating and sanctioning it (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/#:~:text=,Economic%20Stability%2C%20and%20Democratic%20Harmony">INTEL KARTEL – BRIEF INTEL BRIEFINGS</a>) (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/#:~:text=">INTEL KARTEL – BRIEF INTEL BRIEFINGS</a>). These articles mix ideological fear-mongering (e.g. “<strong>weaponization of orphan networks</strong>”) with action plans (tasking HUMINT/SIGINT) in absurd fashion.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Unique or Notable Strategies:</strong> Several proposals stand out as highly unconventional:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>“Autonomous Defender Zone”</strong>: One strategy suggests carving out <em>10% of Ukraine</em> as a military-autonomous region for Zelensky’s loyal forces, with its own governance and economy (legal cannabis, high-tech industries) to reward veterans and sustain defense (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/intelco/#:~:text=">DIASPORA INTELCO – INTEL KARTEL</a>) (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/intelco/#:~:text=cultivation%2C%20pharmaceuticals%2C%20and%20logistical%20services,financial%20recovery%20and%20geopolitical%20stability">DIASPORA INTELCO – INTEL KARTEL</a>). This “state-within-a-state” idea is presented as ensuring long-term victory.</li>
<li><strong>Social Media Targeting (“Operation Social Selection”)</strong>: A satirical briefing proposes that modern warfare can only hit people <em>“we know”</em> via social media profiles. It humorously argues anonymity makes you “immune” and even plans psyops to force targets back online (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/target-list-writing/#:~:text=Due%20to%20advancements%20in%20data,as%20anonymity%20effectively%20creates%20immunity">– TARGET LIST WRITING – INTEL KARTEL</a>). (This mock-brief lists a Hungarian “Telefontanú” hotline ad and then details absurd operations based on Facebook data.)</li>
<li><strong>Crowd-Sourced Surveillance:</strong> Beyond child tracking, the site imagines using crowds to police criminals (hotlines, community watch) and even “pinnacle” crowdsourcing data (e.g. public facial recognition or matching). These emphasize using collective tech for security, at the expense of privacy.</li>
<li><strong>Mass Fortification for Defense:</strong> The Orbán proposal’s 240,000 bunker-homes illustrates an extreme defense strategy. It envisions entire communities housed in hillside bunkers along borders (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/lobby-proposal-to-prime-minister-orban-viktor-and-the-hungarian-people-3.pdf#:~:text=1.%20Hat%C3%A1rv%C3%A9delem%20meger%C5%91s%C3%ADt%C3%A9se%3A%20A%20Pann%C3%B3niai,modern%20lak%C3%B3terekkel%20%C3%A9s%20integr%C3%A1lt%20v%C3%A9delmi">Lobby Proposal to Prime Minister Orbán Viktor and the Hungarian People</a>), blending civilian housing with military base.</li>
<li><strong>Funding Social Programs via Elite Citizenship Sales:</strong> The idea of auctioning citizenships to fund universal basic income is highly novel. Proposing $90B from wealthy immigrants (3 M USD passport price) to pay every Hungarian 50 000 HUF weekly (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/lobby-proposal-to-prime-minister-orban-viktor-and-the-hungarian-people-3.pdf#:~:text=%E2%97%8F%20Ad%C3%B3kedvezm%C3%A9nyek%20az%20IKEA%20%C3%A9s,vez%C3%A9relt%20j%C3%B6v%C5%91re%2C%20amely%20%C3%B6tv%C3%B6zi%20a">Lobby Proposal to Prime Minister Orbán Viktor and the Hungarian People</a>) combines economic and migration policy in one scheme.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Evidence of Bias or Ideology:</strong> The content shows a mix of ideological influences and clear biases:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Anti-Extremism and Anti-War:</strong> The author frequently denounces both left-wing and right-wing extremes. Posts scream “<strong>End the far left and end the far right!</strong>” and <strong>“Who is weaponizing our teenagers?”</strong> (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/2023/12/17/khazar-nobel-winners/#:~:text=WHO%20IS%20WEAPONIZING%20OUR%20TEENAGERS,AND%20WHAT%20FOR">INTEL 56 – INTEL KARTEL</a>), suggesting a populist “middle” stance. Simultaneously, there is a strong anti-war bent – for instance he bluntly writes <em>“A háború illegális… és kurvára nagyon.”</em> (“War is illegal… damn right.”) (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/magyar-politikai-nezetek/#:~:text=A%20HABORU%20ILLEGALIS%20%2C%C2%A0%2056%2C%C2%A0ES,KURVARA%20NAGYON">DANI (VD) POLITIKAI NEZETEI – INTEL KARTEL</a>). The focus on preserving “Christian and European” Hungarian identity (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/lobby-proposal-to-prime-minister-orban-viktor-and-the-hungarian-people-3.pdf#:~:text=az%20integr%C3%A1ci%C3%B3b%C3%B3l%2090%20milli%C3%A1rd%20doll%C3%A1ros,heti%2050%20000%20forintos%20t%C3%A1mogat%C3%A1ssal">Lobby Proposal to Prime Minister Orbán Viktor and the Hungarian People</a>) also shows nationalist overtones.</li>
<li><strong>Conspiratorial Tone:</strong> Many reports allege vast hidden schemes (child-trafficking gangs, global “shadow” networks, sabotage of democracy). This indicates a conspiracist worldview. The language in the site (“weaponization of X”, “shadow governance”, etc.) and some content (fantastic patents, psychic control tech) reflect suspicion of elites and technology.</li>
<li><strong>Satirical and Populist Style:</strong> The writing often uses slang and Internet memes (e.g. profanity, joking phrases). Proposals like <em>“Put granny and the kids in weed farms!”</em> or <em>“Thursday money”</em> are phrased sarcastically or with crude humor (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/magyar-politikai-nezetek/#:~:text=CSUTORTOK%20PENZT%21">DANI (VD) POLITIKAI NEZETEI – INTEL KARTEL</a>) (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/magyar-politikai-nezetek/#:~:text=KENDER%20LEGALIZACIO">DANI (VD) POLITIKAI NEZETEI – INTEL KARTEL</a>). This suggests the site is as much theatrical performance as policy.</li>
<li><strong>Possible Ideological Leanings:</strong> The founder’s background (studied counter-terrorism in the US) and praise for strong defense hint at pro-Western security views. However, there are also strong welfare-state ideas (UBI, universal housing) in his vision. The overall mix is idiosyncratic: anti-establishment, anti-authoritarian (on some axes), yet seemingly aligned with a generic “freedom” narrative.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><strong>Author and Affiliations:</strong> The site is authored by <strong>Dániel Vidos (VD)</strong>. He is identified as IntelKartel’s editor-in-chief (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/lobby-proposal-to-prime-minister-orban-viktor-and-the-hungarian-people-3.pdf#:~:text=Vidos%20D%C3%A1niel%20vagyok%2C%20az%20IntelKartel,t%C3%B6r%C3%B6k%2C%20hogy%20megv%C3%A1ltsam%20a%20vil%C3%A1got">Lobby Proposal to Prime Minister Orbán Viktor and the Hungarian People</a>). He claims to have studied counter-terrorism in America and lived abroad seven years (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/lobby-proposal-to-prime-minister-orban-viktor-and-the-hungarian-people-3.pdf#:~:text=2%20Tisztelt%20Orb%C3%A1n%20Viktor%21%20Vidos,t%C3%B6r%C3%B6k%2C%20hogy%20megv%C3%A1ltsam%20a%20vil%C3%A1got">Lobby Proposal to Prime Minister Orbán Viktor and the Hungarian People</a>). On social media he titles himself “Writer and Painter” (his LinkedIn lists IntelKartel and Earlham College). He also releases music under “IntelKartel Inteligencia” on SoundCloud. Politically, he appears linked to a Hungarian group called “Racionalista Rend” (Rationalist Order); the site’s menu and Facebook mention this. The site’s manifesto asserts an “individualist society” vision where every person has their own “kingdom” (house) (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/lobby-proposal-to-prime-minister-orban-viktor-and-the-hungarian-people-3.pdf#:~:text=Elk%C3%A9pzel%C3%A9sem%20egy%20individualista%20t%C3%A1rsadalom%2C%20ahol,hogy%20a%20h%C3%A9tv%C3%A9g%C3%A9t%20is%20f%C3%BCggetlen%C3%BCl">Lobby Proposal to Prime Minister Orbán Viktor and the Hungarian People</a>). In summary, Vidos presents himself as a young, unconventional strategic thinker combining artistic and intelligence interests.</li>
<li><strong>Credibility and Reception:</strong> The site has <strong>no obvious mainstream recognition</strong> or validation. It is not cited by news media or academic sources. Its own disclaimer (dark humor) suggests it is not a serious official channel (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/core-doksi/#:~:text=THIS%20WEBSITE%20IS%20DARK%20HUMOR,WEBSITE%20ABOUT%20THINGS%20THAT%20MAKE">CORE DOKSI – INTEL KARTEL</a>). Much of the content – especially sensational statistics (e.g. <em>“12–28 year olds commit 80% of violent crime”</em> (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/2023/12/17/khazar-nobel-winners/#:~:text=12,OF%20VIOLENT%20CRIME">INTEL 56 – INTEL KARTEL</a>)) – has no verifiable evidence and appears fabricated or taken out of context. The blending of factoids with outlandish fiction (weaponized orphans, life-extension patents, etc.) means claims must be treated skeptically. We found <em>no external critiques or reviews</em> of IntelKartel; it appears to be a self-published blog for a niche audience. In short, its credibility is low: it’s essentially an ideologically driven, conspiratorial fantasy site rather than a reliable source. For example, even the authorship page candidly calls it humor (<a href="https://intelkartel.com/core-doksi/#:~:text=THIS%20WEBSITE%20IS%20DARK%20HUMOR,WEBSITE%20ABOUT%20THINGS%20THAT%20MAKE">CORE DOKSI – INTEL KARTEL</a>). Users should regard its “intelligence briefings” as entertainment or propaganda, not factual analysis.</li>
</ul>
