<li>Rogue elements within law enforcement or private security</li>
<li>Technological units employing neuroweaponry or experimental systems</li>
<li>Civilian contractors operating under covert directives</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">You are formally advised: <strong>NOT ON THIS.</strong></p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">I assert my rights under applicable national and international law, including those enshrined in human rights, digital privacy, and bodily autonomy statutes.</p>

<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Legal Counsel</strong><br/>I will secure legal representation to assess, document, and pursue appropriate action against violators.</li>
<li><strong>Public Documentation</strong><br/>All suspicious or unlawful actions will be publicly logged, archived, and shared with trusted third parties.</li>
<li><strong>Freedom of Information Requests</strong><br/>I will file FOIA and equivalent disclosure requests to uncover activities involving my identity, data, or biometric signature.</li>
<li><strong>Formal Complaints to Oversight Bodies</strong><br/>Submissions will be made to internal affairs, civilian oversight boards, human rights commissions, and ombudsman offices.</li>
<li><strong>Medical and Psychological Evaluation</strong><br/>Any effects suspected to stem from unlawful targeting will be professionally documented for evidentiary purposes.</li>
<li><strong>Technological Countermeasures</strong><br/>I reserve the right to implement all legal protective technologies to detect, block, or neutralize unlawful surveillance or transmission.</li>
<li><strong>International Reporting</strong><br/>Violations may be submitted to organizations including Amnesty International, the UN Special Rapporteur on Torture, and the European Court of Human Rights.</li>
<li><strong>Community Awareness Campaigns</strong><br/>I will educate and alert others to similar tactics and abuses in accordance with lawful activism and civil resistance.</li>
<li><strong>Civil and Criminal Proceedings</strong><br/>I am prepared to initiate lawsuits or press charges, as applicable, against individuals or organizations found in violation of the law.</li>
<li><strong>Peaceful Assertion of Autonomy</strong><br/>I affirm my sovereign, lawful right to live without harassment, coercion, or manipulation. I will stand firmly in that right using every nonviolent, constitutional tool available.</li>
</ol>
