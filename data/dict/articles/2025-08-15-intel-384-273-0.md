<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>INTELLIGENCE MEMORANDUM</strong><br/><strong>Subject:</strong> Misuse of Sound-Emitting Technology for Covert Influence Operations in Hungary</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Classification:</strong> Confidential</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Summary:</strong><br/>Information has emerged indicating that advanced sound-emitting technology—capable of interacting with implanted devices—is being misused within Hungary. Reports suggest that the technology is being applied to influence individuals’ decisions, compel participation in questionable activities, and undermine political affiliations.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Details:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>The system in question appears to be an evolution of Soviet-era “acoustic influence” or “low-frequency” technology, reportedly modified and deployed by foreign-linked actors operating inside Hungary.</li>
<li>Frequencies around <strong>20 Hz</strong>—which are below human hearing threshold but can have neurological and psychological effects—are allegedly being directed at specific individuals (“Targeted Individuals”).</li>
<li>The targets are reportedly encouraged or coerced into taking “silly” or harmful actions, undermining their autonomy and judgment.</li>
<li>These operations appear to disproportionately affect individuals with left-wing political leanings. Given that Hungary’s left-wing support is at historically low levels (approx. 1%), there is concern that such operations are designed to neutralize remaining political opposition and identify new targets.</li>
<li>Allegations also link the perpetrators to violent right-wing groups, including “kill squads” and “death squads,” potentially operating from Hungary to suppress socialist and progressive activity.</li>
<li>The practice, if confirmed, represents a serious violation of human rights, political freedoms, and sovereignty.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Risks:</strong></p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Domestic Stability:</strong> Continued use of such technology could further polarize Hungarian society, silence political dissent, and normalize clandestine violence.</li>
<li><strong>Foreign Influence:</strong> Manipulation by external actors threatens Hungary’s independence in political decision-making.</li>
<li><strong>Public Health:</strong> Chronic exposure to infrasound or covert neurological interference may cause physical and psychological harm.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Recommendations:</strong></p>
<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Immediate investigation by neutral scientific and security bodies into the alleged use of infrasound and implant-interfacing devices.</li>
<li>Protective measures for political minorities, journalists, and activists.</li>
<li>International monitoring to deter the misuse of legacy Soviet Bloc technology for political repression.</li>
<li>Public awareness campaigns to empower citizens to resist covert manipulation and foreign interference.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Conclusion:</strong><br/>If substantiated, these activities constitute a serious security threat, combining psychological warfare, political suppression, and potential human rights abuses. Strong countermeasures—both domestic and international—are necessary to safeguard democratic freedoms and protect vulnerable populations.</p>
