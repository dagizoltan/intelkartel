Or guys. Or alive, really.</p>



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph">Through a complex plan of military mismanagement, misguided ethics, and a sprinkling of questionable fashion choices, we will eliminate the “heavyweights” (both physically and mentally), leaving behind a utopia of tie-dye enthusiasts and financial insolvency.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Phase 1: Let’s Get the Tough Guys to Kill Each Other (The Egenism Exercise)</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Objective:</strong> Get all the big, tough guys—who’ve spent years preparing to fight for our freedom—together in one arena and convince them to handle things with swords, because why not bring medieval solutions to futuristic problems?</li>



<li><strong>Expected Outcome:</strong> The strong, heavily armed, well-trained soldiers of tomorrow will obliterate each other, proving that the real victory lies not in survival, but in scoring style points on TikTok while swinging blades.</li>



<li><strong>Secondary Objective:</strong> Encourage soldiers to bring back phrases like “Those who lift the sword shall die by the sword” because nothing screams “future-forward” quite like ancient clichés.</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Phase 2: Non-Lethal Weapons Department (a.k.a. The Hippie Drafting System)</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Objective:</strong> The Pentagon’s brainchild to combat overpopulation and existential boredom, this program seeks to recruit the gay, the broke, and anyone else who can’t afford avocado toast.</li>



<li><strong>Preferred Candidates:</strong> Ideally, anyone whose Netflix account still runs on their ex’s subscription and those who’ve never quite figured out how to file taxes properly.</li>



<li><strong>Mission:</strong> Send them into battle with “non-lethal” weapons, such as foam swords, giant Nerf guns, and the harsh realization that credit card debt doesn’t disappear with the apocalypse.</li>



<li><strong>Expected Outcome:</strong> After a few rounds of embarrassing themselves on the frontlines, they will return either “enlightened” or too busy asking for financial aid to organize any kind of resistance.</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Phase 3: Destroy America From Within (Negster Forces)</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Objective:</strong> Infiltrate American society with “Negster Forces” (definition still pending), which we assume means deploying moody teenagers and internet trolls to destabilize the nation through passive-aggressive social media posts, subpar TikTok dances, and poorly-executed latte art.</li>



<li><strong>Tactical Advice:</strong> Encourage the infiltration of Starbucks locations nationwide, ensuring no cappuccino foam is safe.</li>



<li><strong>Long-Term Goals:</strong> Once America is emotionally wrecked, financially drained, and desperate for a sense of purpose that can’t be found in the bottom of a Pumpkin Spice Latte, we swoop in.</li>
</ul>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Phase 4: Resurface as the Ultimate Alliance (KaukaZUSA)</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Objective:</strong> After America collapses like a badly baked soufflé, Europe, Russia, and the one remaining guy who insists on doing CrossFit in a war zone will form the ultimate, unsupervised alliance.</li>



<li><strong>Code Name:</strong> <em>KaukaZUSA</em> – Because we needed a name that sounds just confusing enough to keep conspiracy theorists busy for decades.</li>



<li><strong>Future Vision:</strong> The last survivors will live in a weed-smoking, easy-going society where nothing ever gets done, but no one cares because all the toilets work and everyone looks great in rainbow-colored tunics.</li>
</ul>



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Concluding Remarks:</strong><br>The <em>Fully Enclosed System</em> offers a bold, innovative way to reshape the world’s power dynamics through heavy reliance on bad ideas, societal collapse, and the inexplicable durability of Crocs™.
