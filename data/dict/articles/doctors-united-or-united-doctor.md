<h3 class="wp-block-heading">🐷 <strong>THE RICHARD SCARRY WORLD ORDER: A MANIFESTO OF MOTHER-POWER, MEDICAL DOMINANCE, AND GLOBAL EQUALITY</strong></h3>

<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Nepotism isn’t corruption here—it’s survival strategy.</strong> The families with the most access to schools, doctors, and placement power win the long game.</p>
