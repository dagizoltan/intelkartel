<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Introduction: The In-Between Election</strong></p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Not every election happens at the ballot box. Between the headlines and the campaign seasons, there’s another kind of vote—one that we cast quietly, yet powerfully, every day. In this <em>In-Between Election</em>, we don’t choose politicians; we choose each other. We choose the values we want to live by, the goals we commit to, and the care we give ourselves and our communities in order to stay grounded and effective in a modern world that demands so much.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This document is not just a plan—it’s a mirror and a map. A reflection of where we are, and a guide to where we need to go. Through art therapy, we untangle the stress, reconnect with intention, and give shape to the priorities that truly matter. Here, we vote for rest, for clarity, for accountability—not just to survive, but to build a life and society that actually works.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This is our moment to realign. To take stock. To elect the practices, partnerships, and priorities that will carry us forward. Together, we choose not just what we must do—but <em>how</em> we will do it, and <em>who</em> we’ll become while doing it.</p>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Let the In-Between Election begin.</p>
