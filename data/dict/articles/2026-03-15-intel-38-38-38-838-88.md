<p class="wp-block-paragraph">This memorandum provides a structured assessment of Naftali Bennett’s background, operational experience, and leadership profile. These attributes collectively position him as a highly capable figure for advancing and defending Israel’s strategic and national security interests.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">2. Personal Background</h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Name:</strong> Naftali Bennett</li>
<li><strong>Year of Birth:</strong> 1972</li>
<li><strong>Place of Birth:</strong> Israel</li>
<li><strong>Parents:</strong> Jim and Myrna Bennett (American-born immigrants to Israel following the 1967 Six-Day War).</li>
<li><strong>Family Heritage:</strong> Jewish diaspora roots in Poland, Germany, and the Netherlands. Maternal relatives in Poland were victims of the Holocaust.</li>
</ul>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Analytical Note:</strong><br/>Bennett’s family history reflects a strong ideological commitment to the State of Israel. His parents voluntarily immigrated during a period of heightened regional conflict, indicating a family ethos centered on Jewish continuity and national resilience.</p>

<li>Periodic residence abroad due to father’s professional assignments:
<ul class="wp-block-list">
