<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Doctrine of Harshest Punishment for the Use of Psychic or Psi Weapons on Populations</strong></p>



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Preamble</strong><br>We, the undersigned, recognizing the grave and unmitigated threat posed to humanity by the use of psychic (Psi) weapons, assert that such actions represent an unparalleled violation of human rights, public safety, and the fundamental principles of law and order. The deployment of Psi weapons, which exploit the inherent vulnerabilities of the human mind and body, constitutes an act of extreme aggression and control that undermines the integrity of society and the autonomy of individuals. Therefore, it is hereby declared that the use of Psi weapons on any population shall be met with the harshest possible punishment under the law, including but not limited to permanent imprisonment, eradication of privileges, and in the gravest of cases, capital punishment.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Article I: Definition of Psi Weapons</strong><br>For the purposes of this doctrine, the term “Psi weapons” refers to any form of technology, method, or application of psychic or mental influence designed to manipulate, control, harm, or interfere with the thoughts, emotions, or physical states of individuals or groups. This includes but is not limited to telepathy, telekinesis, psychic hacking, and any device or technique capable of remotely affecting the brain or nervous system.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Article II: Classification of Offenses</strong><br>The use of Psi weapons shall be categorized into the following classes of offenses:</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Class I – Mind Control and Thought Manipulation:</strong><br>Any intentional or reckless attempt to manipulate, alter, or control the thoughts, beliefs, decisions, or free will of individuals or groups through psychic means shall be considered a Class I offense. This includes coercion, brainwashing, or the imposition of false memories.</li>



<li><strong>Class II – Physical Harm via Psi Weapons:</strong><br>The intentional use of Psi weapons to cause physical harm, mental degradation, or permanent injury to an individual or population, including but not limited to debilitating pain, destruction of physical or neurological function, or death, shall be classified as a Class II offense.</li>



<li><strong>Class III – Mass Psychic Attack or Genocide:</strong><br>The deployment of Psi weapons on a large scale targeting a community, ethnic group, political population, or the general populace in an attempt to control, subjugate, or eradicate that group constitutes a Class III offense. This includes the use of Psi weapons as instruments of terror or as a means of destabilizing governments, economies, or social orders.</li>
</ol>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Article III: Scope of Punishments</strong><br>The punishments for the use of Psi weapons shall be severe, in direct proportion to the magnitude and consequences of the offense committed:</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Punishment for Class I Offenses</strong><br>Those found guilty of Class I offenses—such as manipulating the thoughts or free will of individuals—shall face permanent imprisonment in a high-security, isolated facility, with full revocation of civil rights, including voting, freedom of speech, and access to public platforms. The individual will remain under constant surveillance, and any future technological means of psychic influence will be permanently disabled.</li>



<li><strong>Punishment for Class II Offenses</strong><br>For the infliction of physical harm, mental destruction, or life-threatening conditions via Psi weapons, the offender shall face life imprisonment without parole, a penalty reflecting the irreversibility of the damage caused. If the offense results in the permanent disability or death of a victim, the offense shall be classified as a “gravely heinous crime” and punishable by execution.</li>



<li><strong>Punishment for Class III Offenses</strong><br>For crimes of mass manipulation, subjugation, or genocide through Psi weapons, the use of such weapons shall be deemed an act of war against humanity. The punishment shall be death, executed in the most decisive and public manner possible to demonstrate the unwavering stance of the global community against such atrocities. Convicted individuals or leaders who deploy Psi weapons on a national or global scale shall face trial by an international tribunal, with judgment rendered in accordance with the principle of universal justice.</li>
</ol>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Article IV: Extradition and International Cooperation</strong><br>Governments, in cooperation with global legal institutions, shall ensure the swift extradition of individuals suspected of committing Psi weapon-related crimes to the jurisdiction of international tribunals or national courts. No safe harbor shall exist for individuals engaging in such heinous acts, regardless of political, economic, or social status. International cooperation will be prioritized to prevent the proliferation and deployment of Psi weapons.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Article V: Responsibility of States and Governments</strong><br>States are obligated to safeguard their populations from the threat of Psi weapons, ensuring that no individual or organization is allowed to possess or develop such means of harm. Governments failing to enact and enforce strict bans on the creation, use, and trade of Psi weapons will be held accountable through diplomatic, economic, and military means.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Article VI: Prevention and Education</strong><br>To deter the potential development and deployment of Psi weapons, it is crucial to implement robust prevention and educational programs, informing the public of the dangers posed by such technologies. International monitoring bodies shall conduct regular checks and develop countermeasures against illicit Psi weaponry, ensuring that individuals do not possess or develop the means to inflict harm using psychic technologies.</p>



<p class="has-text-align-justify wp-block-paragraph"><strong>Conclusion</strong><br>In conclusion, the use of Psi weapons on populations is a transgression of the highest order, a direct assault on human dignity, autonomy, and safety. The international community must stand united in ensuring that the development, possession, and use of such weapons are strictly outlawed and met with the harshest penalties to guarantee the continued peace, freedom, and security of all people. The message is clear: the use of Psi weapons against humanity will be met with the most severe and unrelenting measures, in defense of the fundamental rights and freedoms that define the dignity of life itself.</p>
