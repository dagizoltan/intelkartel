<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>DATE</strong>: [Insert Date]<br><strong>REPORTING OFFICER</strong>: Agent Dark Humor, Division of Satirical Operations</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>MISSION OVERVIEW</strong><br>The recent successful termination of potential threats (codename: <em>Unwanted Fetus</em>) has been a critical step toward the long-term objective of global population control. Operative [Friend’s Name] has set a precedent with an innovative approach to resolving overpopulation, resource shortages, and future societal upheaval.</p>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>OBJECTIVE</strong><br>Phase II of Operation Population Reduction requires the following actionable steps:</p>



<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Replacement of Industrialists with Orphans</strong>: To destabilize the top 1%, high-level industrialists are to be forcibly swapped with peasants or orphans. Objective: Observe how quickly they collapse under the weight of their own incompetence.</li>



<li><strong>Initiation of the Orphan Uprising</strong>: A strategic force composed of lower-class orphans will hunt down and “interview” former elites. Results will be published for public consumption in major propaganda outlets, resulting in mass uprisings.</li>



<li><strong>Mandatory Reproductive Control</strong>: Abortion will be mandatory for the lower classes to prevent further overpopulation. Operative [Friend’s Name] has already been a key contributor to this initiative. Orphanages will be shuttered to eliminate any future opposition from rebellious offspring.</li>



<li><strong>Final Solution – Global Child Depopulation</strong>: The long-term goal is to eradicate the surplus of poor children, particularly focusing on vulnerable populations in Africa and India, via the tactical deployment of disease vectors disguised as “aid.” All resistance will be met with decisive extermination.</li>
</ol>



<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity">



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>INTELLIGENCE SUMMARY</strong><br>Operative [Friend’s Name] is advised to continue “population control efforts” as part of the overarching mission to establish a utopia for the deserving few. This innovative approach to depopulation serves as a blueprint for future operations.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>NOTES</strong>:<br>Casualties of conscience are expected to be minimal. Collateral damage will be ignored in favor of strategic gains.</p>



<p class="wp-block-paragraph"><strong>NEXT STEPS</strong></p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
