<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Here are the Ten Commandments in <strong>Russian</strong>, <strong>English</strong>, <strong>Hebrew</strong>, and <strong>Hungarian</strong>, followed by an explanation of why they are good principles to follow.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">1. <strong>Ten Commandments in Four Languages</strong></h3>
<h4 class="wp-block-heading">Russian:</h4>
<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>Да не будет у тебя других богов перед лицом Моим.</li>
<li>Не делай себе кумира и никакого изображения.</li>
<li>Не произноси имени Господа Бога твоего напрасно.</li>
<li>Помни день субботний, чтобы святить его.</li>
<li>Почитай отца твоего и мать твою.</li>
<li>Не убий.</li>
<li>Не прелюбодействуй.</li>
<li>Не кради.</li>
<li>Не произноси ложного свидетельства на ближнего твоего.</li>
<li>Не желай дома ближнего твоего; не желай жены его, ни раба его, ни рабыни его, ни вола его, ни осла его, и ничего, что у ближнего твоего.</li>
</ol>
<h4 class="wp-block-heading">English:</h4>
<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>You shall have no other gods before Me.</li>
<li>You shall not make for yourself a carved image.</li>
<li>You shall not take the name of the Lord your God in vain.</li>
<li>Remember the Sabbath day, to keep it holy.</li>
<li>Honor your father and your mother.</li>
<li>You shall not murder.</li>
<li>You shall not commit adultery.</li>
<li>You shall not steal.</li>
<li>You shall not bear false witness against your neighbor.</li>
<li>You shall not covet your neighbor’s house, wife, servant, ox, or anything that belongs to your neighbor.</li>
</ol>
<h4 class="wp-block-heading">Hebrew (Transliterated):</h4>
<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li>לֹא יִהְיֶה לְךָ אֱלֹהִים אֲחֵרִים עַל פָּנָי.</li>
<li>לֹא תַעֲשֶׂה לְךָ פֶסֶל וְכָל תְּמוּנָה.</li>
<li>לֹא תִשָּׂא אֶת שֵׁם יְהוָה אֱלֹהֶיךָ לַשָּׁוְא.</li>
<li>זָכוֹר אֶת יוֹם הַשַּׁבָּת לְקַדְּשׁוֹ.</li>
<li>כַּבֵּד אֶת אָבִיךָ וְאֶת אִמֶּךָ.</li>
<li>לֹא תִרְצָח.</li>
<li>לֹא תִנְאָף.</li>
<li>לֹא תִגְנֹב.</li>
<li>לֹא תַעֲנֶה בְּרֵעֲךָ עֵד שָׁקֶר.</li>
<li>לֹא תַחְמֹד בֵּית רֵעֶךָ, לֹא תַחְמֹד אֵשֶׁת רֵעֶךָ, וַעֲבָדוֹ וַאֲמָתוֹ, שׁוֹרוֹ וַחֲמֹרוֹ וְכֹל אֲשֶׁר לְרֵעֶךָ.</li>
</ol>
<h4 class="wp-block-heading">Hungarian:</h4>

<li>Ne csinálj magadnak faragott képet.</li>
<li>Ne vedd hiába az Úrnak, a te Istenednek nevét.</li>
<li>Megemlékezzél a nyugalom napjáról, hogy megszenteljed azt.</li>

<li>Ne paráználkodj.</li>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">2. <strong>Why Following the Ten Commandments Is a Good Idea</strong></h3>
<ol class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Respect for Higher Values</strong>: The first few commandments remind us to value principles and honor commitments beyond ourselves, which fosters a sense of humility and purpose.</li>
<li><strong>Encourages Focus on Positive Behavior</strong>: Prohibiting idolatry and misuse of sacred values keeps us grounded in meaningful priorities.</li>
<li><strong>Promotes Rest and Reflection</strong>: Observing a day of rest is essential for mental, physical, and spiritual well-being.</li>
<li><strong>Strengthens Family Bonds</strong>: Honoring parents creates a foundation of respect and gratitude, ensuring generational continuity.</li>
<li><strong>Safeguards Life</strong>: Prohibiting murder establishes the sanctity of life and security within communities.</li>
<li><strong>Preserves Marital Integrity</strong>: By discouraging adultery, these principles help maintain trust and loyalty in relationships.</li>
<li><strong>Protects Property</strong>: Prohibitions against theft and covetousness foster fairness and discourage jealousy, which are keys to community harmony.</li>
<li><strong>Upholds Honesty</strong>: Avoiding false witness is vital for trust and justice within society.</li>
<li><strong>Nurtures Gratitude</strong>: The commandment against envy helps people appreciate what they have instead of resenting others.</li>
</ol>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Following these guidelines helps build trust, foster healthy relationships, and create a stable, peaceful society. They are timeless principles for personal integrity and communal well-being.</p>
