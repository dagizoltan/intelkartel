<h2 class="wp-block-heading">🧑‍⚖️ Péter Magyar: From Fidesz Insider to Opposition Leader</h2>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">Péter Magyar, born in 1981 in Budapest, is a lawyer and former diplomat. He was a member of the ruling Fidesz party from 2002 until early 2024. His departure from Fidesz was catalyzed by internal disagreements and public dissatisfaction with the government’s direction. In March 2024, Magyar took over the relatively obscure Tisza Party and transformed it into a prominent political force. Under his leadership, the party secured 29.6% of the vote in the 2024 European Parliament elections, winning 7 seats and becoming the leading opposition party in Hungary .(<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/P%C3%A9ter_Magyar?utm_source=chatgpt.com">Wikipedia</a>, <a href="https://hungarynewsinenglish.com/p/hungarys-main-political-parties-2025?utm_source=chatgpt.com">Hungary News In English</a>)</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">🏛️ The Tisza Party: Ideology and Objectives</h2>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">The Tisza Party, officially known as the Respect and Freedom Party (Tisztelet és Szabadság Párt), positions itself as a center-right, conservative, and pro-European political entity. Its core objectives include:(<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tisza_Party?utm_source=chatgpt.com">Wikipedia</a>)</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Restoring Western Alliances</strong>: Magyar has pledged to realign Hungary with NATO and the European Union, countering Orbán’s perceived drift towards autocratic regimes like Russia and China .(<a href="https://apnews.com/article/10300218f9a6fa87b05d19538fe3a798?utm_source=chatgpt.com">AP News</a>)</li>
<li><strong>Anti-Corruption Measures</strong>: The party advocates for robust anti-corruption policies and increased transparency in governance.</li>
<li><strong>Media Freedom</strong>: Tisza opposes legislation that threatens press freedom and civil society, standing against laws that could suppress independent media .(<a href="https://www.theguardian.com/world/2025/may/21/eu-urged-to-act-over-hungarian-legislation-which-could-restrict-free-press?utm_source=chatgpt.com">The Guardian</a>)</li>
<li><strong>Social Policies</strong>: The party emphasizes improvements in healthcare, education, and support for rural communities, aiming to address issues like teacher shortages and underfunded health services .(<a href="https://infostart.hu/belfold/2025/01/21/magyar-peter-ha-magyar-erdekkel-szemben-kellene-szavazni-a-tisza-part-felfuggeszti-a-tagsagat-a-neppartban?utm_source=chatgpt.com">infostart.hu</a>)</li>
<li><strong>Agricultural Support</strong>: Magyar has expressed intentions for Tisza to become a representative party for small farmers, filling the void left by the dissolution of previous agrarian parties .</li>
</ul>
