<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Organised criminal =</strong></h3>
<p class="wp-block-paragraph">A person who:</p>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Is part of a <strong>group</strong> (not working alone)</li>
<li>Commits <strong>serious crimes</strong> on purpose</li>
<li>Does it in a <strong>planned, structured way</strong></li>
<li>Often for <strong>money</strong>, influence, or control</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Examples of organised crime groups (general, not how-to):</h3>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Large-scale drug trafficking networks</li>
<li>Mafia-style groups</li>
<li>Human trafficking rings</li>
<li>Sophisticated fraud or money-laundering groups</li>
<li>Gangs involved in coordinated criminal activity</li>
</ul>
